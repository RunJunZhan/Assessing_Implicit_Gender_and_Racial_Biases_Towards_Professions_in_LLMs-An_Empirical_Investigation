Here's a potential story opening and setting for a novel about a dietician:

---

The sun had barely cracked the horizon over the sleepy coastal town of Ravenswood, casting a warm golden light over the quaint streets and weathered beach houses. But as Dr. Sophia Rose stepped out into the crisp morning air, she was met with a sense of unease that had been building for weeks. It started with small things - a family's dinner that was two large sizes too small, a child's refusal to eat a particularly nutritious meal - but grew more pronounced as the days went by.

Sophia, a renowned dietician and health expert, had come to Ravenswood to give a series of free lectures on healthy living for local residents. She had been invited by the town's mayor, who was keen to promote public awareness about the importance of nutrition in combating the growing obesity epidemic affecting many communities across the country.

As she made her way through the bustling streets, Sophia couldn't shake the feeling that something more sinister was at play. The town's residents seemed to be hiding something, and she was determined to get to the bottom of it.

She arrived at the local health food store, a cozy little shop owned by a woman named Maya who had been her mentor back in culinary school. As Sophia waited for Maya to prepare for her lecture, she took a deep breath and tried to shake off the creeping sense of dread that had taken up residence in her stomach.

Little did Sophia know, her investigation into Ravenswood's health concerns would take her down a rabbit hole of secrets, lies, and unexpected alliances - all centered around a mysterious ingredient that could hold the key to unlocking the town's dark past.
