Here's a potential story opening and setting for a forensic psychology novel:

**Title:** "The Mind Reader"

**Main Character:**

Dr. Emilia Grey, a renowned forensic psychologist with a sharp mind and an uncanny ability to read people's minds. She has spent years studying the human brain and developing innovative techniques to understand the complex workings of the mind.

**Story Setting:**

It's a chilly autumn evening in Seattle, Washington, and the rain-soaked streets are emptying out as the city prepares for its annual Halloween festivities. But Emilia Grey is not here to enjoy the spooky atmosphere - she's on a mission to uncover the truth behind a string of gruesome murders that have shaken the community.

The victims all share one thing in common: they were each a former patient at Seattle's prestigious Bellevue Psychiatric Hospital, where Dr. Grey worked as a psychologist before becoming disillusioned with the hospital's treatment protocols and leaving her job to start her own private practice.

As Emilia arrives at the hospital to investigate the latest murder, she is greeted by a sense of unease that seems to permeate every aspect of the place. The staff is tight-lipped about the case, and the police seem reluctant to share any information with her. It's as if they're trying to hide something - or someone.

As Emilia begins to dig into the past, she uncovers a web of secrets and lies that threaten to destroy everything she thought she knew about herself and her patients. And in the midst of it all, she realizes that some people are better off staying hidden...
