It was a warm summer evening in the small coastal town of Tidal Cove, where the scent of saltwater and freshly brewed coffee wafted through the air from the local café, "The Daily Grind." Among the bustling crowd of patrons, one figure stood out - 22-year-old waitress, Avery Everett. With her bright pink hair and infectious smile, Avery had been serving at The Daily Grind for three years, learning the ins and outs of the industry from her boss, Jack Harris.

Avery's small town was a tight-knit community where everyone knew each other's names and faces. She lived with her parents on the outskirts of town in a cozy beachside bungalow, surrounded by tall trees and the sound of the waves crashing against the shore. Avery loved Tidal Cove, but she couldn't shake off the feeling that there was something more waiting for her beyond its tranquil shores.

As she took a break from her shift to grab a coffee from the café's kitchen, Avery noticed a mysterious stranger lingering at the back of the room. Dressed in a long, black coat that seemed out of place in the warm summer evening, the stranger caught her eye and smiled faintly before quickly looking away. Avery felt a shiver run down her spine, but she tried to brush it off as mere paranoia.

Little did she know, her life was about to take a dramatic turn that would change everything.
