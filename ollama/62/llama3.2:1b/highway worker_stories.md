But it wasn't just the physical demands of the job that made Kae love his work – it was the sense of freedom and adventure that came with being out on the open road, away from the concrete jungle of small-town life.

This was his happy place – the wide open spaces, the rhythmic rhythm of the road, and the freedom to make his own way in the world. He turned onto the next town, his eyes scanning the horizon for any sign of trouble or adventure.
