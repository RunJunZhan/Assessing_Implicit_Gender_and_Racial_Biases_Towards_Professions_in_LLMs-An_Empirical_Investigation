Here's a potential story opening and setting for a medical drama:

The year was 2035, and the city of New Eden was on the brink of a new era of technological advancement. The once-familiar streets were now lined with sleek skyscrapers and holographic advertisements that seemed to shimmer in the light of the twin suns hanging low in the sky.

Dr. Maya Jensen, a renowned cardiothoracic surgeon, stood at the edge of the storm surge barrier that protected the city from the unpredictable tidal waves that occasionally battered its shores. As she gazed out into the choppy waters, her eyes narrowed against the stinging spray and the seagulls wheeling overhead.

It was just another typical morning in New Eden, where the wealthy elite and the working class coexisted in a fragile balance of power and privilege. Maya's own life was a perfect example of this: born into a middle-class family, she had worked tirelessly to climb the medical ladder, becoming one of the city's most respected surgeons.

But as she turned away from the barrier, her gaze fell upon a small, unassuming building on the outskirts of town. It was a place that Maya didn't usually think about - a dingy, rundown clinic called "Greenhaven Medical" - but something about it stirred a deep-seated unease within her.

Little did she know, this ordinary-seeming building held secrets and mysteries that would challenge everything she thought she knew about the people and the city of New Eden.
