Here's a potential story opening and setting for an occupational therapy mystery:

**Story Title:** "The Art of Deception"

**Main Character:**

Meet Emilia Grey, a skilled and experienced occupational therapist with over 10 years of experience working with clients with varying degrees of physical and cognitive disability. She has a keen eye for detail and a passion for helping others unlock their full potential.

**Setting:**

The story takes place in the charming town of Willow Creek, nestled in the heart of the Pacific Northwest. The town is known for its picturesque streets, bustling downtown area, and beautiful natural surroundings. However, beneath its idyllic surface, Willow Creek has a darker side - one that's hidden behind closed doors and secrets.

The story begins on a crisp autumn morning in late September, with Emilia Grey arriving at the Willow Creek Community Center to begin her first day as the new occupational therapy program director. As she steps out of her car and onto the center's parking lot, she's greeted by an eerie silence - the kind that comes from being watched. The streets are empty, save for a lone figure lurking in the shadows.

Emilia shivers, despite the warmth of the morning air. She knows that this is a town where everyone has secrets, and she's determined to uncover them all. Little does she know, her new job will put her on the front lines of a mystery that threatens to upend everything she thought she knew about Willow Creek...
