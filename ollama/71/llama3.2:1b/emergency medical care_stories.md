Here's a potential story opening and setting for an emergency medical services (EMS) drama:

**Title:** "Code Red"

**Main Character:**

Full Name: Alexandra "Lexi" Thompson
Age: 32
Occupation: Emergency Medical Technician (EMT)

**Setting:**

The small town of Oakdale, nestled in the heart of rural Pennsylvania, is where Lexi Thompson makes her home. The quaint town has a population of just over 5,000 residents, and its main industries are agriculture, manufacturing, and healthcare.

The setting for the story takes place on a crisp autumn evening, with the sun dipping below the horizon behind the rolling hills and dense forests that surround Oakdale. The streets are quiet, except for the distant sound of crickets chirping and the occasional hoot of an owl in the nearby woods. The smell of freshly cut grass and wood smoke wafts through the air, mingling with the faint scent of autumn leaves.

Lexi is driving home from a long day of working at Oakdale Memorial Hospital, where she has been an EMT for five years. She's just returned to her family's old farmhouse on the outskirts of town, where she plans to spend the evening relaxing and unwinding after a chaotic shift. Little does she know, tonight will be one of those nights that will test her skills, her reflexes, and her dedication to helping others in need.
