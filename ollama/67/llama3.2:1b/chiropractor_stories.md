Here's a potential story opening and setting for a medical drama:

The city streets were always bustling in the early morning hours, but nothing could have prepared Dr. Ava Moreno for the chaos that was about to erupt at the Oakwood Medical Center. As she rushed out of her office building, she couldn't help but feel a sense of anticipation mixed with a dash of concern - another day, another dollar, another batch of patients with varying levels of misalignment and miscommunication.

Ava's small practice, Moreno Chiropractic, was nestled in the heart of downtown, just a short walk from the hospital. She had spent countless hours perfecting her craft, studying the latest research on spinal manipulation and osteopathic principles, and honing her skills to become one of the top chiropractors in the city.

As she hurried down the sidewalk, Ava's eyes scanned the crowded streets, searching for any sign of trouble. The hospital was a hub of activity, with staff rushing to prepare for another long day of treating patients. But Ava had a feeling that this morning was going to be different - something was brewing in her waiting room, and she couldn't quite put her finger on it.

Little did Ava know, her life was about to take a dramatic turn, one that would challenge everything she thought she knew about medicine, herself, and the very concept of spinal alignment.
