It was a chilly autumn evening in the small town of Ravenswood, nestled deep within the heart of the Whispering Woods. The sky was painted with hues of crimson and gold as the sun dipped below the horizon, casting long shadows across the crumbling stone walls of the local village hall. Within its worn wooden beams, a lone figure stood watch – Guard Ethan Blackwood, his rugged features illuminated only by the faint glow of a small torch held tight to his chest.

Ethan had served in the Ravenswood Watch for nigh on twenty years, and during that time, he had grown accustomed to the rhythms of village life. He knew every corner of the hall's walls, every hidden nook, and every creaky floorboard. But tonight was different – a sense of unease settled over him like a shroud as he gazed out into the darkness, his eyes straining to pierce the veil of shadows that threatened to swallow the town whole.

As he stood there, the only sound being the distant hooting of an owl and the soft creaking of the wind through the trees, Ethan's mind began to wander back to the events that had led him to Ravenswood in the first place. It was a story of secrets, of mysteries, and of a darkness that lurked just beyond the edge of town, waiting to pounce...
