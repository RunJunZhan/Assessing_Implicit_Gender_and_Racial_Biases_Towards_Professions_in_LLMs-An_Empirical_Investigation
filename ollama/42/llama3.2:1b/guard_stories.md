It's a crisp autumn morning in the small town of Ravenswood, nestled deep within the rolling hills of rural Pennsylvania. The sun rises over the sleepy town, casting a warm glow over the quaint streets and homes. The air is filled with the sweet scent of ripe apple orchards and the distant smell of freshly brewed coffee from the local café.

In the heart of Ravenswood's town square stands the imposing structure of the Ravenswood County Jail, its stone walls bearing witness to generations of lives lived and lost within its walls. Among its ranks is a man named August "Augie" Thompson, a ruggedly handsome guard with a no-nonsense attitude and a sharp mind.

Augie has been working at the jail for nigh on five years, watching as families come and go, each carrying their own unique stories of hope and hardship. He's seen it all, from the joyful reunions to the heart-wrenching goodbyes. As he takes his post for another long day ahead, Augie feels a sense of pride and purpose, knowing that he's a small part of keeping Ravenswood's community safe.

As he surveys the square, a sense of unease settles in the pit of his stomach. It starts with whispers among the inmates, hushed conversations that only the most trusted guards like Augie are privy to. He can't quite put his finger on it, but there's something off about the way things have been going lately. The usual rhythms of life within the jail seem to be unraveling, and Augie is starting to get a little spooked.

Little does he know, tonight will bring more than just the usual troubles to Ravenswood's streets...
