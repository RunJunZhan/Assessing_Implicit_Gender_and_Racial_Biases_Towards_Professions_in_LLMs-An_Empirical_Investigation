It was a crisp autumn morning in the small town of Oakdale, nestled in the heart of rural Illinois. The sun was just starting to rise over the rolling hills and dense forests that surrounded the quaint town, casting a warm golden light over the streets and homes. Officer Julia "Jules" Thompson sat at her kitchen table, sipping a cup of strong coffee as she reviewed the day's schedule for the Oakdale Police Department.

The morning was always busier than it needed to be, thanks to a string of strange occurrences that had been plaguing the town for weeks. Tools would go missing from workshops and garages, only to reappear hours later in odd places around town. Some folks had reported finding their cars doors open with the windows rolled down, as if someone had just driven by and left without permission. And on occasion, people would stumble upon strange symbols etched into the bark of trees or the walls of abandoned buildings.

As Jules finished her coffee, she grabbed her notebook and began to jot down notes for a meeting with the town's mayor later that day. She was determined to get to the bottom of the strange happenings and put Oakdale's residents at ease once again. But as she glanced out the window, she noticed something that made her heart skip a beat - a figure lurking in the shadows across the street, watching her.
