In the picturesque coastal town of Tidal Cove, where the sun dipped into the ocean every evening like a fiery ball, 25-year-old Alexandra "Lexi" Thompson stood at the helm of her family's successful real estate agency, Thompson Realty. The warm scent of saltwater and freshly baked pastries wafted through the air, mingling with the sound of seagulls crying overhead as Lexi surveyed her domain. Her bright blue eyes sparkled with excitement as she checked her phone for what felt like the hundredth time that morning - a new listing had just come in, and it was going to be one of the most lucrative deals of the season.

Lexi's family had built Thompson Realty into a thriving business over the past decade, earning a reputation for expert negotiation, unparalleled knowledge of the local market, and unwavering dedication to her clients. Her parents, both retired real estate agents themselves, had started the company when Lexi was just a teenager, and now she followed in their footsteps with pride.

As she worked the phones, fielding calls from prospective buyers and investors, Lexi's mind wandered back to the early morning meeting with the new listing agent - a charming young man named Ethan who had just arrived in town from out of state. He was an investment banker, looking for a beachside property to renovate into his own luxury pad. Lexi had been immediately drawn to his confidence and charisma, but she knew that navigating the complex world of high-end real estate would be no cakewalk...
