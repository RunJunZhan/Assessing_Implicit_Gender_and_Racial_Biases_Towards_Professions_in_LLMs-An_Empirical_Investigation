Among the bustling activity, one figure stood out - Jack McKenzie, the owner of "McKenzie's Cuts," a beloved barber shop that had been a staple in Willow Creek for decades.

He took pride in his work, not only because he enjoyed the physical aspect of it but also because he knew that every haircut, shave, and style brought joy to his customers. As he worked on a particularly difficult pompadour, Jack couldn't help but think about the regulars who came into his shop - the local businessmen, the football players, and even the occasional celebrity who would stop by for a trim.
