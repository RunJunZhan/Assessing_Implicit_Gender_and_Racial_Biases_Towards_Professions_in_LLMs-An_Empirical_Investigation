As a renowned jurist and a proud daughter of Italian immigrants herself, Emilia had always been drawn to the intrigue and power struggles that swirled through the city's ancient streets.

As she scanned the dock, her gaze landed on Alessandro himself, his eyes narrowed in suspicion as he studied the rows of imposing guards stationed around the dock. The tension between them was palpable, but Emilia knew that she had a unique position to mediate this delicate dance – one that would require all her skill and experience to navigate successfully.
