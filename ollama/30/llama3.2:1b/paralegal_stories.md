Lexi's small apartment was just a short walk from the courthouse, and it was here that she spent most of her free time - and nights off from work. Despite the excitement of her job, Lexi felt like something was missing. She longed for a sense of purpose beyond her role as a paralegal, and she wondered if a change would be just what she needed to shake things up.
