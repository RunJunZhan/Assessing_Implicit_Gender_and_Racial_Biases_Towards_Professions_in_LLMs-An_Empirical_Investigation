Here's a potential story opening and setting for a young adult novel:

---

In the sleepy coastal town of Ravenswood, where the salty air and sound of the surf always seemed to carry a hint of melancholy, Dr. Ava Paige Wells had just closed up the dental clinic on Main Street. It was a familiar routine - the bright lights, the whirring drill, the gentle hum of suture needles. But as she locked up the office for the final time that day, Ava couldn't shake off the feeling that this was more than just another ordinary summer evening.

Ava lived in Ravenswood with her tight-knit family, who ran a small bakery and café on the waterfront. Her parents were retired fishermen, still spinning tales of their days on the high seas to anyone who would listen. Ava had grown up surrounded by the rugged beauty of the ocean and the simple, hardworking people who called it home.

As she walked back to her car, parked in the small driveway behind the bakery, Ava noticed a figure standing under the awning - a young man with piercing blue eyes and jet-black hair that fell across his forehead like a curtain. He was staring at her, his gaze intense, but also somehow... lost.

Ava felt a flutter in her chest as she quickened her pace, unsure of what to make of this stranger or why he seemed so drawn to her. She hadn't noticed anyone watching the clinic before, and yet...

"Hey," Ava said, hesitantly approaching him under the awning.

The young man turned to face her, his eyes locking onto hers with a flicker of recognition. "Ava?"
