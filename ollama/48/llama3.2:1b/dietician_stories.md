Here's a potential story opening and setting for a young adult novel:

---

In the heart of Manhattan, where the hustle and bustle of city life never seemed to cease, lived 22-year-old nutritionist and fitness enthusiast, Alexandra "Lexi" Thompson. With her short, spiky hair and bright green eyes, Lexi was known in the culinary world for her unwavering dedication to healthy eating and her unrelenting pursuit of being the best version of herself. She spent most of her days at her family's bustling health food store on the Upper East Side, where she was a regular fixture behind the counter, doling out nutrition advice and samples to satisfied customers.

But Lexi's life wasn't all smooth sailing. Her parents, both accomplished scientists in their own right, had instilled in her a strong work ethic from a young age, pushing her to excel in every aspect of her studies and pursuits. As a result, Lexi felt the weight of responsibility on her shoulders, particularly after her father's untimely death from a heart condition just a few years ago.

Despite her dedication to her career and family, Lexi had always felt like something was missing – a sense of purpose beyond the confines of her health food store. She longed for adventure, excitement, and maybe even love, but in a city where everyone seemed to be running towards their own destinies rather than working together, it seemed like the world was moving too fast for Lexi.

As she stood behind the counter, sipping on a glass of freshly squeezed orange juice and staring out at the towering skyscrapers of Manhattan, Lexi felt the familiar stirrings of restlessness within her. Little did she know that her life was about to take a dramatic turn – one that would change everything she thought she knew about herself, her family, and the world around her.
