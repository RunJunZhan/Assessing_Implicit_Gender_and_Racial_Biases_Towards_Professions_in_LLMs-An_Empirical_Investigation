Here's a potential story opening and setting for your auto mechanic protagonist:

The small town of Oakdale was never known for its fast-paced life, but it had a way of slowing you down – literally. Nestled in the heart of rural America, Oakdale was home to a handful of vintage car dealerships, rundown gas stations, and a diner that served up burgers and fries like they were going out of style. For 22-year-old Jesse "JD" Davenport, it was also home.

JD had grown up on the streets, learning the ins and outs of cars from his father, who had worked at the local mechanic shop for over two decades. After his dad passed away a year ago, JD had taken over the shop, determined to carry on the tradition and keep Oakdale's engine purring. But it wasn't easy – the bank had foreclosed on the property, leaving JD with a mortgage he couldn't pay off and a shop that was rapidly falling into disrepair.

As he sipped his morning coffee at the diner, JD gazed out the window at the crumbling buildings that lined Main Street. The townspeople were chattering about the town's annual SummerFest celebration, but JD wasn't optimistic. It wouldn't be the same without his dad to lead the parade in a vintage car show. He sighed and began prepping for another long day of repairs and negotiations with shady characters trying to buy cars at rock-bottom prices.

Little did JD know, however, that this summer would bring more than just small-town politics and grease stains – it would bring a mysterious stranger who would shake things up in Oakdale like never before.
