Ruby took a deep breath, mentally preparing herself for another long shift, as she gazed out into the crowded pub, her mind wandering to the plans she'd made for the night: a quiet dinner with her best friend in the city, followed by a solo show at an open mic night in the neighborhood.

McGillicuddy's Pub had been Ruby's home away from home for as long as she could remember. It was more than just a bar – it was a sanctuary, a community of regulars who knew her and trusted her to pour their drinks without judgment. As she expertly flipped a strand of lettuce onto the rim of the glass, Ruby felt a sense of comfort wash over her, but it was quickly replaced by the nagging feeling that tonight wouldn't be so quiet after all...
