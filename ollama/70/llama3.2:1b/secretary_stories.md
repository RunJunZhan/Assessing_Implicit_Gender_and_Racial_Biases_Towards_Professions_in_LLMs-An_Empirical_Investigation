The sounds of car horns, chatter, and construction work filled the air, providing a constant background hum that was both familiar and comforting.

It was just another ordinary Monday morning in the office, but Emily's thoughts were already turning to her afternoon meeting with her supervisor, Mrs. Thompson, who had promised to brief her on some new project details.
