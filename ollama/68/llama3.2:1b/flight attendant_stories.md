She loved this job, not just because it allowed her to travel and see new places every week, but also because it gave her a chance to make a difference in people's lives - whether it was by providing a comforting word or simply offering a helping hand during a difficult time.

As she took her seat at the front of the cabin, Lexi felt a sense of excitement and anticipation - it was going to be another busy flight, but she was ready for it.
