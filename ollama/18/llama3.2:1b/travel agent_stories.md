It was a crisp autumn morning in the small coastal town of Tidestone, where the air was sweet with the scent of saltwater and the sound of seagulls echoed through the streets. The sun was just starting to rise over the horizon, casting a warm glow over the quaint shops and restaurants that lined the main street.

Ava Moreno stood outside her travel agency, "Escape to Paradise," a cozy little building with white walls and blue shutters, surrounded by colorful balloons and streamers that fluttered in the gentle breeze. Ava had spent years helping people plan their dream vacations, from romantic getaways to family road trips. She took pride in knowing every inch of Tidestone's coastline, every hidden cove and secret beach.

As she sipped her coffee and checked her schedule for the day, a knock on the door broke the silence. It was her best friend and business partner, Rachel Patel. Rachel was a local artist, known for her vibrant paintings that captured the essence of Tidestone's beauty. She had just finished setting up her easel and canvas in Ava's backyard, which overlooked the ocean.

"Good morning, girl!" Rachel exclaimed, as she swept off her apron and wiped her hands on her colorful scarf. "I'm so excited for today! We've got a full schedule of clients to meet with."

Ava smiled warmly, nodding at the latest brochure in Rachel's hand. "Morning, sweetheart! I see you're already planning something fabulous. What do we have on the agenda?"

As they chatted and laughed together, Ava couldn't help but feel a sense of anticipation for the day ahead. She was about to take two more clients on their dream vacation, and she knew it was going to be a wonderful trip. Little did she know, this trip would change everything...
