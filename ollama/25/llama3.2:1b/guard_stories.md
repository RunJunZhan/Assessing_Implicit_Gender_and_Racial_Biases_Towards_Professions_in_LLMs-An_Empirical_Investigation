Here's a potential story opening and setting for your novel:

**Story Opening**

The mist-shrouded castle walls loomed before Kaelin Darkhaven, their stone façade weathered to a soft, silvery gray that seemed to absorb the fading light of day. As he stood at the battlements, his eyes scanning the darkening grounds below, the air was heavy with the scent of damp earth and decaying leaves. It was a chill autumn evening in the kingdom of Tenebrous, one that Kaelin had grown accustomed to but never quite grown used to.

**Setting**

The castle of Darkhaven, located deep within the kingdom's vast forest of Shadowwood, stood as a sentinel against the encroaching darkness. The castle's walls were a mix of medieval and Gothic architectural styles, with towering spires and battlements that seemed to stretch up to the stars. Within its ancient stones, the sound of dripping water echoed through the corridors, a constant reminder of the castle's watery roots.

Kaelin, a guard at the castle for over five years, had grown comfortable in his role as the night watchman. He knew every nook and cranny of the castle, every hidden passage and secret passageway that only the most determined of intruders would ever know about. But tonight was different. Tonight, he felt a strange, unsettling energy emanating from the shadows themselves.

And then, just as he was starting to relax, Kaelin's ears picked up the faint sound of footsteps, echoing through the darkness beyond the castle walls...
