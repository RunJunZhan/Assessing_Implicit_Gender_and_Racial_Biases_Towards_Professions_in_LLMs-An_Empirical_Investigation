Augie's hands moved with a practiced ease, expertly coiling his scissors as he listened intently to the gossip of the morning: the latest rumors about the town's newest family arrival, the whispers of a long-lost relative reappearing after decades away, and the cryptic warnings about an impending storm that promised to bring more than just the usual summer chaos.

As Augie worked, his mind wandered back to his childhood in Ravenswood, where he had grown up watching the sunsets paint the sky with hues of pink and gold.
