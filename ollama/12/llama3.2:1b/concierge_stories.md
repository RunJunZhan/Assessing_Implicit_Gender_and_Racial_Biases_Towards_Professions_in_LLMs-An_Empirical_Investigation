As she stood at the front desk of the Grandeur Hotel, greeting the first wave of arrivals with a practiced flourish, Emily felt a sense of pride and purpose wash over her. And as the sun began to set over the towering skyscrapers, casting a golden glow over the bustling streets below, Emily couldn't help but feel that this was exactly where she belonged.
