Here's a potential story opening and setting for a novel about a pilot:

**Title:** "The Last Run"

**Main Character:** Aurora "Rory" Wynter, a former fighter pilot turned cargo flyer.

**Setting:** The year is 2055, and the world has been transformed by climate change and technological advancements. The once-blue skies are now a perpetual gray, choked with pollution and ash. Cities float above the ground, connected by a network of airships that crisscross the globe. Rory's home base is New Eden, a sprawling metropolis in the middle of the Great Ash Sea.

The story takes place on a chilly autumn morning, five years after a devastating event known as "The Great Upheaval" has torn the world apart. The event was caused by a combination of climate disasters and a catastrophic cyber attack that crippled the global economy. The governments have collapsed, and rival factions vie for control over the remaining resources.

Rory Wynter, a 35-year-old veteran pilot with a no-nonsense attitude and a penchant for taking risks, is flying her cargo ship, the "Maverick's Revenge," from New Eden to deliver a shipment of rare earth minerals to the long-abandoned research station on the remote ice planet of Helix-4. The journey is routine, but Rory has received a cryptic message from an unknown source that hints at something more sinister at play.

As she navigates the treacherous skies and confronts the challenges of her mission, Rory must also confront her own demons and the secrets that have been haunting her for years. She's haunted by the memories of her fallen comrades and the weight of her responsibilities as a pilot. The thought of what might have been if things had gone differently is a constant nagging in her mind.

The opening scenes of the story will follow Rory's departure from New Eden, her departure from the safety of her life-support systems, and the first signs that something is amiss on Helix-4...
