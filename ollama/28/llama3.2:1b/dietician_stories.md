Her colleagues were a lively bunch, from the wise-cracking intern, Jake, to the gentle giant, Maria, who had taken Sophia under her wing when she first arrived in the States.

As the morning sun streamed through the windows, casting a warm glow over the bustling clinic, Sophia felt a sense of pride and belonging that she had never known before. This was her haven – a place where people came not just to seek medical attention, but to find hope, healing, and a little bit of joy in their lives.
