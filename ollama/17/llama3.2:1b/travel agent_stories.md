It was a crisp autumn morning in the small coastal town of Tidestone, where the sun rose over the waves to greet the day like a warm hug. The air was alive with the sweet scent of saltwater taffy and freshly brewed coffee, wafting from the bustling local café on Main Street. This was Emily Thompson's hometown, and she took pride in her role as the owner of Thompson Travel Inc., a small but beloved travel agency that had been a staple in Tidestone for over 20 years.

As Emily sipped her morning coffee and scanned the newspaper, her eyes widened at the headline: "Tidestone Hosts Intergalactic Tourism Festival." Her heart skipped a beat as she read on, learning that this year's festival would feature space-themed excursions, astronaut lectures, and even a planetary disco party. Emily had always dreamed of organizing such an event, and now it seemed her chance was finally within reach.

With her trademark infectious enthusiasm, Emily picked up the phone to start making calls to potential attendees and vendors. She was the mastermind behind Thompson Travel Inc., and this festival would be the perfect opportunity for her agency to shine – both on Earth and in the cosmos. Little did she know, her big idea would not only bring people together but also attract a whole new crowd of travelers from far-flung corners of the galaxy...
