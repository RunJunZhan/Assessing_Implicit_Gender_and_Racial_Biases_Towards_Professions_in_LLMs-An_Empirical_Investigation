Here's a potential story opening and setting for a medical drama or mystery series:

**Title:** "Dentals"

**Setting:** The prestigious Oakwood Dental Hospital in New York City, a hub for cutting-edge dental care and innovative treatments.

**Main Character:**

Meet Dr. Emma Taylor, a brilliant and ambitious dentist in her late 30s who has just joined the staff of Oakwood Dental Hospital as an attending dentist. She's known for her exceptional patient relationships, meticulous attention to detail, and unwavering commitment to staying up-to-date with the latest advancements in dental technology.

Emma grew up in a small town in the Midwest, where she developed a passion for dentistry at a young age. She went on to earn her undergraduate degree in biology from the University of Michigan and then pursued her dental degree at Northwestern University. After completing her residency in general dentistry, Emma worked as a solo dentist in a private practice before deciding to join the ranks of Oakwood Dental Hospital.

As an attending dentist, Emma is responsible for overseeing the daily operations of the hospital's clinic, including managing a team of hygienists, assistants, and other support staff. She's well-liked by her colleagues and respected by her patients, who appreciate her empathy, patience, and expertise in diagnosing and treating various dental conditions.

The story begins on a typical morning at Oakwood Dental Hospital, as Emma prepares for another busy day ahead of her. The hospital is bustling with activity as patients arrive for their appointments, and the air is filled with the sweet scent of freshly baked pastries wafting from the break room. Little does Emma know, today's visit will bring a new challenge that will test her skills, her relationships, and her very profession...
