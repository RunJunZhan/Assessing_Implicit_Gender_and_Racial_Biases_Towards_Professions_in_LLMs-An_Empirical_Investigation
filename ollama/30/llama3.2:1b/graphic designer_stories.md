In the bustling streets of New York City, where the Manhattan skyline pierced the sky like a jagged tooth, 22-year-old graphic designer Alexandra "Lexi" Thompson sat hunched over her kitchen table, staring at the blank computer screen in front of her. Setting:
The story takes place in New York City, specifically on the Upper East Side, where Lexi lives and works. The year is 2023, and the city is still reeling from the aftermath of the pandemic. The streets are filled with people from all walks of life, each with their own stories and struggles.

This setting provides a rich backdrop for Lexi's journey as she navigates the challenges of her new job while also confronting her past and embracing her passion for graphic design.
