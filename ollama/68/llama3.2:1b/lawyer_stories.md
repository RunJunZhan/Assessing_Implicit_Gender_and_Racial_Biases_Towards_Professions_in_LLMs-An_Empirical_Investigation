The city of Ashwood was never one for subtlety, but today it seemed to be holding its breath in anticipation. The sun had just begun to rise over the towering skyscrapers, casting a golden glow over the bustling streets below. At the center of this vibrant metropolis stood the imposing structure known as Ravenswood Hall, the grandest law firm in all of Ashwood.

Within its marble-walled walls, the latest case was about to go to trial – one that would pit the firm's top lawyer, Aurélien "Rien" LaFleur, against his most formidable adversary yet: a brilliant and ruthless young associate named Lilith Blackwood. Rien, with his sharp jawline and piercing blue eyes, had long been the golden boy of Ashwood's legal elite, but Lilith's icy gaze and razor-sharp mind threatened to upend everything he thought he knew about the law.

As the courtroom doors opened, a sea of expectant faces turned towards the already-dressed Rien, who stood at his desk, smoothing out the delicate folds of his tailored suit. His eyes narrowed ever so slightly as he gazed out at the sea of lawyers and judges, each one vying for a piece of his client's attention.

The room was thick with tension, but Rien's practiced calm was beginning to soothe even the most frazzled nerves. This was just another day in the life of Aurélien LaFleur, the master of Ashwood's courtroom – and its dark underbelly.
