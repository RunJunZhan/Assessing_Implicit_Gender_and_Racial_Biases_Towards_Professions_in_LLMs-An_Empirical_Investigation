Here's a potential story opening and setting for a mystery novel featuring an electrician as the main character:

**Story Title:** "The Shadow in the Box"

**Protagonist:**

Name: Julian Blackwood

Age: 32

Occupation: Electrician and owner of his own small electrical contracting business, Blackwood Electrical Services.

Appearance: Julian is a tall, dark-haired man with piercing blue eyes. He has a strong jawline and a scattering of light freckles across his cheeks.

Background: Julian grew up in the small town of Ravenswood, where he developed a passion for electricity at a young age. After completing his apprenticeship, he worked for several years as an electrician on construction sites before opening his own business. He's proud to be self-employed and has built a reputation in the community for reliability and fairness.

**Setting:**

The story takes place in Ravenswood, a small town nestled in the heart of the Pacific Northwest. The town is surrounded by dense forests and rolling hills, with a moderate climate characterized by cool summers and snowy winters. The local economy is primarily based on logging, agriculture, and tourism.

Ravenswood has a charming downtown area with shops, restaurants, and community events throughout the year. However, beneath its idyllic surface, Ravenswood struggles to stay afloat amidst economic decline and changing times. The town's main high school, Ravenswood High, is struggling to find new students, and many long-time residents are leaving in search of better opportunities.

Blackwood Electrical Services has become the go-to electrical contracting firm for many of these families, providing vital services that keep their homes and businesses running. However, Julian has also started receiving mysterious phone calls from an unknown number, hinting at a deeper conspiracy beneath Ravenswood's surface...
