Here's a potential story opening and setting for an Emergency Medical Technician (EMT) named Alexandra "Lexi" Thompson:

It was a chilly autumn evening in the small town of Oakdale, Illinois. The streets were empty, save for the occasional flash of headlights as residents pulled out to grab a late-night slice of pizza or catch a game at the local bar. The only sound was the distant hum of the highway, which ran along the outskirts of town like a snake slithering through the underbrush.

Lexi Thompson adjusted her scuffed-up EMT jacket and grabbed her bag, a knapsack full of medical supplies and a worn-out coffee thermos that she knew would be warm by the time she arrived at Station 17. She had been an EMT in Oakdale for five years now, and this was supposed to be a quiet night. But as she made her way to the station, Lexi couldn't shake off the feeling that something was brewing.

As she pulled into the parking lot, she noticed a dark sedan parked at the edge of town, its engine running but its headlights not on. The driver's side window rolled down, revealing a pair of piercing blue eyes and a chiseled jawline that made Lexi do a double take. "Hey," he said, his voice low and smooth as silk. "Can I help you?"

Lexi felt her heart skip a beat as she replied, "Uh, yeah... I'm here to check on some patients downtown."
