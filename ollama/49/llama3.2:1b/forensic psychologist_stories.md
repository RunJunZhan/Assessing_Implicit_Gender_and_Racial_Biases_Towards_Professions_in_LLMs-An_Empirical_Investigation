A worn, leather-bound chair sat in one corner, where Sophia had spent countless hours pouring over case files and developing her expertise in the field of forensic psychology.

Sophia's office was located on the top floor of the psychiatric hospital, overlooking the city streets below. The view was breathtaking, but it also served as a constant reminder that the world outside these walls was full of people just like those she studied – flawed, complex, and often desperate for help.

Sophia's team was already bustling around the office, preparing for another long day of evaluating, diagnosing, and treating the next individual who would come through their doors.
