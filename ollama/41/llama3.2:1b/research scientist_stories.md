A warm breeze rustled the leaves of a particularly large specimen, its bark adorned with strange symbols that Evie was determined to decipher. Her long, curly brown hair was tied back in a messy bun, revealing a scattering of freckles across her cheeks and nose. She wore a pair of thick, black-rimmed glasses perched on the end of her nose, giving her an intelligent, yet slightly absent-minded appearance.

As she worked, Evie's mind began to wander to the upcoming meeting with Atlas Corporation's representative, Dr. Liam Chen, who had promised to discuss potential collaborations and funding opportunities for her research.
