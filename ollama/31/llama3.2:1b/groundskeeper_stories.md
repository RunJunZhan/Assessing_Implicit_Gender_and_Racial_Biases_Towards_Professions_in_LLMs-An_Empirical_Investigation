It was a crisp autumn morning in the small town of Oakdale, nestled in the heart of the Whispering Woods. The sun was just starting to rise over the rolling hills and green pastures, casting a warm golden light over the quaint streets and homes. In the midst of this peaceful scene, 35-year-old groundskeeper, Emrys "Em" Fletcher, emerged from the rustic oak treehouse that served as his home and office. He stretched his lean frame and yawned, revealing a hint of stubble on his jawline.

As he made his way through the quiet streets, the soft crunch of leaves beneath his feet was the only sound breaking the morning calm. Em had lived in Oakdale all his life, working alongside his father at the local golf course since he was a teenager. After completing his education, he had decided to pursue a career in horticulture, and after years of hard work, he had risen through the ranks to become the groundskeeper.

The Whispering Woods were in full autumnal glory, with the trees donning their finest hues of orange, red, and yellow. The air was filled with the sweet scent of ripe fruit, and the soft chirping of birds could be heard in the distance. It was a tranquil scene that Em took great pride in maintaining, and he spent his days tending to the gardens, fixing equipment, and keeping the grounds in top condition.

As he walked along the winding path that led to the golf course, Em noticed a sense of unease lingering over the usually peaceful landscape. The trees seemed to be leaning in, their branches tangling together like skeletal fingers. The wind rustled through the leaves, causing them to whisper an unsettling melody. Em's instincts told him that something was amiss, but he couldn't quite put his finger on what it might be.
