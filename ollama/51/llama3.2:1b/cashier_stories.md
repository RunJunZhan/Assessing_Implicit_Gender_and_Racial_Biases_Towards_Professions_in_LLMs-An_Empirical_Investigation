It was a typical Wednesday morning at Sunny Side Up Coffee Shop, the sun peeking through the curtains above the counter as it did every day. The aroma of freshly brewed coffee wafted through the air, mingling with the sound of chirping birds and the hum of conversation from the early risers gathered by the window. This was Emily Wilson's first day on the job, but she had been working at Sunny Side Up for a few years now, watching as her coworkers learned and grew into their roles.

As Emily clocked in, the bell above the door jingled, signaling the start of another busy shift. She took a deep breath, smoothing out her apron as she prepared to face the morning's chaos. Her coworker, Rachel, poked her head over the counter to chat before heading back to her station.

"Hey, Em, you gonna get settled in today?" Rachel asked with a grin, eyeing Emily's new uniform.

Emily smiled and shook her head. "Just need to familiarize myself with the register. Trying to learn everything already."

Rachel chuckled and headed back to work, leaving Emily to begin her training for the day ahead.
