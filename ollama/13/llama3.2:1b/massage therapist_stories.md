It was a warm summer evening in the small coastal town of Tidal Cove, where the sound of the waves gently lapping against the shore created a soothing melody that seemed to wash away all worries. In a cozy little clinic nestled among the bustling streets of downtown, Massage Oasis had its home. The sign above the door read "Samantha 'Sam' Thompson" - a skilled massage therapist with a passion for helping her clients unwind and find peace.

Sam sat on the couch in her clinic, sipping a glass of iced tea as she listened to the soothing sounds of the ocean outside. She had just finished a long day of massages and a few private sessions with local business owners who were recovering from injuries. As she worked out the kinks from her own back, Sam felt grateful for this peaceful life in Tidal Cove. But little did she know, tonight was about to bring more than just relaxation - it would be a night that would change everything.
