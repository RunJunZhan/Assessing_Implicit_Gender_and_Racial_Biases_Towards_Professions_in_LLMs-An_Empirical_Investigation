It was a typical Monday morning at Oakwood Dental Care, with the sweet scent of freshly polished toothbrushes and the gentle hum of drills filling the air. Dr. Sophia Patel, a renowned dental surgeon with over a decade of experience, stood behind her counter, sipping her coffee as she reviewed her schedule for the day.

As she scanned the list of patients, her eyes landed on a peculiar name: Ethan Alexander Montgomery. Sophia's curiosity was piqued - who was this mysterious patient, and what could have prompted him to schedule an appointment with her? She made a mental note to do some research before meeting him in the waiting room.

The door swung open, and a tall, lanky man with messy brown hair walked in, adjusting his black leather jacket. He flashed a charming smile as he approached Sophia's counter. "Dr. Patel, thank you for seeing me today."

Sophia's eyes narrowed slightly as she took in Ethan's disheveled appearance - he looked like he hadn't showered in days, and his hair was sticking up in every direction. But there was something about him that seemed...familiar.

"Ah, Mr. Montgomery," Sophia said, her tone friendly but measured. "Please, come on in."
