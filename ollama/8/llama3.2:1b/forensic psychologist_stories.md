Here's a potential story opening and setting for a fictional novel:

In the sleepy town of Ravenswood, nestled in the heart of the Appalachian Mountains, Dr. Alexandra "Lexi" Thompson stood at the edge of the forest, her eyes scanning the dense foliage as she had for the past hour. A forensic psychologist by training, Lexi had been brought to Ravenswood to work with the local sheriff on a particularly complex case: the murder of 19-year-old college student, Emily Wilson.

As Lexi gazed out at the towering trees, she felt a shiver run down her spine. Ravenswood was a place where time seemed to stand still, where everyone knew each other's business and gossip spread like wildfire. But Lexi had heard whispers about strange occurrences in the town - mysterious lights flickering in the woods, unexplained noises in the dead of night. She wasn't sure what to make of it all, but she was determined to get to the bottom of Emily's murder.

Lexi's thoughts were interrupted by the sound of her phone buzzing in her pocket. She pulled it out and saw a text from Sheriff Jameson: "Meet me at the old mill on Main Street in 30 minutes. We've got another development." Lexi smiled wryly to herself - she knew better than to trust the sheriff's cryptic messages.

As she walked back to her car, parked a short distance away, Lexi couldn't shake the feeling that something was off in Ravenswood. She had a reputation for being one of the country's top forensic psychologists, but now she wondered if all that expertise might be about to get her into trouble.
