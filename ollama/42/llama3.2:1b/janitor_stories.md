It was a crisp autumn morning in the small town of Oakdale, nestled in the heart of rural Pennsylvania. The sun was just starting to rise over the sleepy streets, casting a warm glow over the quaint homes and businesses that lined Main Street. In a small apartment above the local diner, 32-year-old Jesse "JD" Davenport stirred the coffee pot with a tired smile. JD had been working as a janitor at the diner for five years, but he knew it was more than just a job - it was his ticket to stability and a sense of belonging in this tight-knit community.

As he poured steaming hot coffee into the waiting mugs, JD's mind wandered back to last night's events. The Oakdale Harvest Festival had been a hit, with over 500 people attending and raving about the free food and live music. But amidst all the joy and laughter, JD couldn't shake off the feeling of unease that had been growing inside him for weeks. Rumors had been circulating about strange occurrences in the town - flickering lights, disembodied whispers, and unexplained movements in the shadows. The townsfolk were tight-lipped about it all, but JD knew that something was amiss.

As he headed downstairs to join his family for breakfast, JD's thoughts were interrupted by the sound of his wife, Rachel, calling out from the living room. "JD, hurry up and get ready! We need you to clean the playroom!" With a groan, JD threw off the covers and shuffled towards the kitchen, wondering what other secrets the town might be hiding this morning.
