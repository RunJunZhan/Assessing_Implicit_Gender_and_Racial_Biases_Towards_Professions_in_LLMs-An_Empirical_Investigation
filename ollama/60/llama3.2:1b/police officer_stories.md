In the sleepy suburban town of Oakdale, nestled between the bustling cities of Chicago and Detroit, Officer Alexandra "Lexi" Thompson stood at the edge of the Grand River, gazing out at the quaint streets and lawns that seemed to stretch on forever. As Lexi took her position at the entrance of the Oakdale Police Department, she felt a familiar sense of calm wash over her. She had been a police officer for five years now, and this small town had become like home to her. Her colleagues knew her as tough but fair, with a quick wit and a sharp mind. But Lexi's own life was about to take an unexpected turn.

As she began to review the day's schedule, a strange noise caught her attention. It sounded like someone – or something – was rumbling through the streets of Oakdale, heading straight for the police station.
