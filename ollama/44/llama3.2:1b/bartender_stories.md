It was a chilly autumn evening in the small town of Willow Creek, nestled between the rolling hills and dense forests of rural America. The sun had long since set, casting a warm orange glow over the quaint streets and homes. Inside the local watering hole, "The Smokey's," bartender and owner, Alexandra "Lexi" Thompson, polished a glass with a white cloth, her eyes scanning the crowded room with a practiced ease.

The air was thick with the smell of sizzling burgers and the hum of lively chatter, as regulars and newcomers alike gathered around the bar to share stories and enjoy the warm atmosphere. Lexi's years of experience had made her expert at reading the crowd, and she knew exactly who to serve what drink to when. She worked with a precision that came from years of practice, her hands moving with a fluidity that belied her years.

As she wiped down the bar with a damp cloth, Lexi's mind wandered back to the strange letter she'd received earlier that morning. It was from an old friend, inviting her to meet him at "The Smokey's" for a drink and a chat. The letter had been unsigned, but the words had seemed to leap off the page: "Come alone." Lexi had always been a bit of a loner, content to keep to herself, but there was something about the letter that had piqued her curiosity...
