The mist-shrouded alleys of Newhaven Castle seemed to writhe and twist around Captain Julian Styles, his eyes scanning the darkness with a practiced intensity that had become second nature after years of service as a guard in the castle walls. The stone facade loomed above him, its towering spires and battlements a testament to the city's rich history, but for Julian, it was home - or at least, as much home as one could expect from a place where time itself seemed to bend and warp.

Born into the family that had long protected Newhaven Castle, Julian had grown up with stories of his ancestors' bravery and sacrifice etched into his very bones. He knew every nook and cranny, every hidden passage and secret room within the castle's imposing walls. It was a life he'd chosen willingly, or at least, he thought he had - after all, who wouldn't want to spend their days patrolling the battlements, watching over the sleeping city below?

But as the years went by, Julian began to feel a growing sense of restlessness, a nagging feeling that there was more to life than the narrow routine of his guard's duties. And so, he'd started to slip away from the castle walls at night, sneaking out into the mist-shrouded streets to explore the city and all its secrets - or so he thought.

Little did Julian know, his nocturnal adventures were about to take a dramatic turn, one that would put his life as a guard and a citizen of Newhaven Castle in very real danger...
