It was a chilly autumn morning in the heart of New Haven, a city famous for its rich history and academic institutions. The year was 1955, and the campus of Yale University was buzzing with activity as students prepared for another busy semester. Amidst the hustle and bustle, one individual stood out among the crowd - 22-year-old janitor, Everett "Ev" Thompson.

Everett lived in a small apartment at the edge of campus, a modest place that he shared with his mother, Georgia. Despite the lack of luxury, Ev had grown up surrounded by the beauty of Yale's architecture and the intellectual fervor of its students. He took pride in his work as a janitor, ensuring that the university's facilities were spotless and welcoming for those who needed them.

As Ev made his way to the main building, he carefully carried a large trash bag filled with the day's recyclables. The crisp morning air was filled with the sweet scent of freshly brewed coffee from the campus café, where students gathered to socialize and study. Ev nodded to the familiar faces as he passed by - there was Sarah, who always brought him a cup of black coffee on her way home; and Alex, who often stopped by to chat about the latest sports game.

Ev's job as a janitor had become an integral part of his life. He took pride in keeping the university running smoothly, and he knew every nook and cranny, every cluttered corner and every dusty shelf. But little did anyone know that Ev had a secret - one that would change everything when he stumbled upon an old, mysterious-looking book hidden deep within the library's archives...
