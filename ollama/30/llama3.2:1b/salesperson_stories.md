It was a crisp autumn morning in the bustling city of New Haven, where the sun rose over the skyscrapers casting long shadows across the concrete streets. The air was alive with the hum of traffic and the wail of sirens, but amidst the chaos, one individual stood out - Alexandra "Lexi" Thompson, a 28-year-old salesperson at the prestigious Oakwood Enterprises marketing firm.

Lexi stepped out of her sleek, silver sedan and onto the pavement, her dark hair tied back in a tight ponytail as she took a deep breath of the fresh morning air. She lived for days like this, when the city was alive with energy and possibility, and she got to put on a happy face for her clients and superiors alike. With a confident smile plastered on her face, Lexi made her way towards the Oakwood Enterprises building, ready to tackle another day of trying to close deals and selling the latest gadgets to the city's business elite.

As she entered the lobby, Lexi was greeted by the friendly face of receptionist Karen Jenkins, who handed her a warm smile and a steaming cup of coffee. "Good morning, Lexi! Looking forward to your meeting with Richard Langley today." Lexi smiled back, feeling a sense of anticipation build in her chest - another big sale was just around the corner, and she was determined to make it happen.
