Lexi stands backstage, surveying the chaos before her. The theater is buzzing with last-minute decisions, technical worries, and creative tension, all swirling together in a perfect storm of artistic expression.

As Lexi begins to make the final adjustments to the set design, she can feel the energy building. She takes a deep breath, mentally preparing herself for the challenges ahead. Tonight's show promises to be one of the most powerful performances of her career, and Lexi is determined to bring it to life.
