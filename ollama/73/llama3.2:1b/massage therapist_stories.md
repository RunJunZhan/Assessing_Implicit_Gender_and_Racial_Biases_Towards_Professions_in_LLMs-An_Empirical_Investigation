Here's a potential story opening and setting for a novel about a massage therapist:

---

The sleepy town of Willow Creek lay shrouded in a perpetual mist, as if the very air was alive with secrets waiting to be unraveled. Nestled between the majestic oak trees that lined its winding streets, the town seemed to hum with an otherworldly energy - a gentle vibration that only came from the heartbeat of its residents.

At the heart of this tranquil oasis beat the rhythms of Emily Mayfield, a licensed massage therapist with a touch as soothing as it was potent. Her cozy wellness center, "The Serenity Spot," stood at the edge of town, where a riot of colors and scents bloomed in her lush garden, enticing passersby to come and let go of their worries.

As Emily worked on her latest client, a young couple struggling to reconcile their differences, the mist outside seemed to thicken, casting an eerie gloom over the scene. The soft chirping of birds and the distant rustle of leaves created an intimate atmosphere, one that allowed the therapist to focus on the intricate dance of muscles and emotions beneath her skilled hands.

Little did Emily know, tonight would bring more than just a tense couple seeking refuge in her massage therapy - it would be a night that would unravel secrets, ignite passions, and set in motion a chain reaction of events that would change the course of their lives forever.
