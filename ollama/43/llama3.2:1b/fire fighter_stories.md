The Storm Within

Ava Ryder was already halfway up the ladder when her radio crackled to life, the voice on the other end shouting her name. "Ryder, we've got a multiple-vehicle accident on Highway 12, about two miles east of town. Reports indicate at least three vehicles are overturned, with several people injured."

Ava's eyes scanned the horizon as she swung her legs over the side of the fire truck and landed softly on the ground. She was already mentally preparing herself for what she knew would be a tough day ahead. As a firefighter with the local department, Ava had seen her fair share of accidents and injuries, but nothing could truly prepare you for the chaos that erupted when nature decided to take its course.

Ava pulled on her worn leather gloves and climbed back into the truck, grabbing her backpack with the essentials - water, first aid kit, and a change of clothes. As she set off towards the accident site, the rain began to pour down, casting a gloomy shadow over the landscape. Ava's firefighter helmet was already secure behind her ears, but she couldn't shake the feeling that today was going to be one of those days where everything seemed to go wrong.

She pulled up to the scene and took a deep breath, scanning the area for any signs of life or damage. The sun had just begun to peek through the clouds, casting an eerie glow over the overturned vehicles. Ava's heart rate quickened as she spotted the first injured person - a young woman, half-buried in the debris.
