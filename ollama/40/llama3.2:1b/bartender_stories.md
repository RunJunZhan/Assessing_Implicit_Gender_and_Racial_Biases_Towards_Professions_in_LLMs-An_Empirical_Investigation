It was a warm summer evening in the small coastal town of Tidestone, where the sun dipped into the ocean, casting a golden glow over the bustling beachside streets. The smell of saltwater taffy and sizzling burgers wafted through the air, mingling with the sound of laughter and music drifting from the local bar, "The Salty Dog".

Ava Moreno polished a glass with a white towel, her dark hair tied back in a ponytail as she surveyed the crowded room. She had been working at The Salty Dog for three years now, and it was a place where everyone knew each other's names and stories. Ava was well-liked by most, known for her warm smile and quick wit. However, little did anyone know that beneath her polished exterior, Ava was struggling to make ends meet.

As she expertly mixed a piña colada, the door swung open, and a tall, dark-haired man walked in, shaking off the rain on his way to the bar. He was a regular at The Salty Dog, and Ava had grown accustomed to his presence. But there was something about him that caught her attention – a sense of intensity, a look that seemed to bore into her very soul.

Ava's gaze locked onto the stranger, and for a moment, they just stared at each other. She felt a flutter in her chest, and her mind began to wander back to the reasons she hadn't left her job three years ago. The truth was, Ava had been burned before – by love, by heartbreak, and by people who promised the world but delivered nothing. But there was something about this stranger that made her want to take a chance on him.

As he ordered a whiskey on the rocks, Ava's thoughts were interrupted by the sound of the door swinging open once again. This time, it was followed by the hum of conversation and the clinking of glasses. The Salty Dog was abuzz with activity, but Ava's attention remained fixed on the stranger, her mind racing with possibilities. Little did she know, their lives were about to become irreparably entwined in ways they couldn't yet imagine...
