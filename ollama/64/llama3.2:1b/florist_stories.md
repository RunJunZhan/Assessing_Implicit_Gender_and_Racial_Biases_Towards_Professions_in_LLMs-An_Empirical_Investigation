On this warm autumn morning, as the sun struggled to break through the misty sky, Luna carefully arranged a bouquet of sunflowers and dahlias on her signature display table, adorned with a handwritten sign that read: "Welcome to Willow Creek – Where Every Day is a Blooming Success."

The shop's cozy interior was lined with vintage flower pots, colorful silk scarves, and an assortment of antique gardening tools. A chalkboard menu listed an array of traditional teas, homemade jams, and small-batch soaps, all crafted by Luna's mother, a skilled artisan.
