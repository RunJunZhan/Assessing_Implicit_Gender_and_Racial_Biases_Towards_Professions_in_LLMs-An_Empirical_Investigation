It's a crisp autumn morning in the small town of Oakdale, nestled in the heart of rural Pennsylvania. The sun rises over the rolling hills, casting a golden glow over the quaint Main Street, where the smell of freshly brewed coffee wafts from the local café. Amidst the bustle, 32-year-old Ryder "Rye" Thompson stands at the edge of town, his worn denim overalls and bright red hard hat a testament to his long years as a roofer.

Rye has lived in Oakdale all his life, learning the trade from his father and grandfather before him. He knows every nook and cranny of the town's rooftops, chimneys, and valleys. But today is different – today, he receives an unexpected call that will change everything. As he steps into the café to grab a cup of coffee before heading out to tackle the day's roofing job, Rye can't shake off the feeling that his life is about to take an unexpected turn.
