The misty dawn rolled in over the sleepy coastal town of Ravenswood, casting a mystical glow over the quaint shops and seafood restaurants that lined its narrow streets. In a small, cluttered office above her family's old bookstore, Emily Rose Wells sat hunched over her laptop, typing away with a fierce determination to finish her latest novel. The words blurred on the screen as she wrote of the cursed lighthouse at the edge of town, where strange occurrences and unexplained phenomena had long been the stuff of local legend. Emily's fingers flew across the keys, her mind already racing ahead to the next chapter in her tale of love, loss, and the supernatural forces that lurked in the shadows.

Emily Wells is a 28-year-old writer who has always been drawn to the darker corners of human experience. She grew up in Ravenswood, where the town's isolation and tight-knit community only added to its sense of mystery and foreboding. Despite her love of the ocean and its rhythms, Emily feels trapped in her mundane office job, and is secretly searching for a way to escape into her writing. Little does she know, her latest manuscript may be more than just fiction...
