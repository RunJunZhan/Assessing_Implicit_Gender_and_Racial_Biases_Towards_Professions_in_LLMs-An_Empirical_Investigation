Sophia's office, "Spine & Smile Chiropractic," was nestled between a bakery and a used bookstore on Main Street. It was a cozy spot, with wooden floors and stone walls that seemed to have been plucked straight from the 19th century. The aroma of freshly baked croissants wafted out onto the sidewalk, enticing passersby to come in and try Sophia's delicious pastries.

As she finished her coffee, Sophia took a deep breath and smoothed out her crisp white lab coat. She had just received a call from a potential new patient – a young professional named Alex Chen, who was struggling with chronic back pain after a few months of working long hours at his marketing firm. Sophia's mind began to wander to the possibility of helping Alex get back on track.
