It was a chilly autumn morning in the small town of Ravenswood, nestled in the heart of the Appalachian Mountains. The misty dawn air clung to the streets like a damp shroud, casting a mystical veil over the quaint town square. In the midst of this serene scene, 25-year-old guard Ethan Blackwood stood watch at the entrance of Ravenswood's oldest and most historic building, the Raven's Peak Inn.

Ethan, a ruggedly handsome man with piercing blue eyes and dark hair, had been working as a guard for the inn for three years. He took his job seriously, ensuring that the town's guests and employees were safe from any potential threats. Ethan was no stranger to the dangers of rural life; he had grown up in Ravenswood, learning the ins and outs of its residents and the local businesses.

As he gazed out into the misty morning, Ethan felt a sense of pride and responsibility wash over him. He knew that today would bring another busy day at the inn, with a mix of tourists and locals bustling about, all vying for his attention. But little did he know, this day would be different, filled with secrets, lies, and unexpected danger that would test his skills as a guardian like never before.
