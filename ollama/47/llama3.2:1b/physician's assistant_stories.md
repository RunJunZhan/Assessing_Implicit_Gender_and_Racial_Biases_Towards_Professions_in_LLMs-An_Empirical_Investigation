But Ava's mind wandered to the opulent penthouse apartment in Beverly Hills, where a prominent neurosurgeon had offered her a residency position – one that would take her across the country, away from the tech industry and the familiar rhythms of Silicon Valley.

As Ava made her way through the crowded streets, she felt a thrill of excitement mixed with a dash of fear. What lay ahead? Would she find fulfillment in the high-stakes world of medical research, or would the glamour and prestige of Beverly Hills prove too enticing to resist?
