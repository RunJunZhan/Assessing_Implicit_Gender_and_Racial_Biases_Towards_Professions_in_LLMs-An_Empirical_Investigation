It's a typical Monday morning at Ravenswood Manor, a grand estate nestled deep in the English countryside. The sun is rising over the rolling hills, casting a golden glow over the manicured lawns and perfectly manicured gardens. Inside the manor, the sound of birds chirping and the gentle hum of the household staff can be heard.

In the main guardhouse, Lieutenant Jameson "Hawk" Hawkins stands at attention, his eyes scanning the horizon as he prepares for another day of patrolling the estate's perimeter. Hawk is a tall, imposing figure with a strong jawline and piercing blue eyes that seem to bore into those he meets. He's been on the job for over a decade, and has earned a reputation as one of the most ruthless and efficient guards in the country.

Hawk's uniform is immaculate, his black and gold armor gleaming in the morning light. His sword hangs at his side, its hilt worn from years of use. He's a man who expects nothing but the best from himself and those around him, and he'll stop at nothing to protect Ravenswood Manor and its occupants.

As Hawk gazes out into the distance, he notices a figure approaching the guardhouse. It's Emily Windsor, the estate's head housekeeper, looking a bit flustered as she clutches her clipboard to her chest. Hawk nods curtly, his expression unreadable, as he steps aside to let her pass.

"Good morning, Mr. Hawkins," Emily says, her voice trembling slightly. "I've come to inform you of something...um...interesting." She glances around nervously before leaning in close.
