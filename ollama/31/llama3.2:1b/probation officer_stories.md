Here's a potential story opening and setting for a mystery/thriller novel:

---

The mist-shrouded streets of Ashwood Cove were always at their most treacherous in the early morning hours. It was as if the town itself was holding its breath, waiting for the dawn to break and reveal the secrets that lay hidden beneath its quiet facade.

That's where Emily Mayfield patrolled the streets as a probation officer, her eyes scanning the darkness with a mixture of caution and curiosity. A graduate of the University of New England's criminology program, Emily had spent years honing her skills in the field, but she still felt like an outsider among the locals who seemed to know each other's business.

As she walked, the mist swirled around her ankles, obscuring everything beyond a few feet. Emily shivered, despite the thick sweater she wore. It was a small thing, but one that had started when she first took up her job. She felt like an outsider here, in Ashwood Cove, where everyone knew everyone and secrets were as safe as the town's picturesque harbor.

The sound of gravel crunching beneath her feet broke the silence, and Emily turned to see a figure emerging from the mist. He was tall and lean, with piercing blue eyes that seemed to bore into her very soul. His dark hair was slicked back, revealing a sharp jawline that made him look almost otherworldly.

"Can I help you?" Emily asked, trying to keep her tone neutral despite the flutter in her chest.

The stranger smiled, his eyes glinting with amusement. "Just checking on some... business," he said, his voice low and smooth. "I'm afraid we need to talk."

Emily's instincts told her to be wary, but something about the stranger's words seemed... off. She hesitated for a moment before nodding towards the mist-shrouded streets behind him.

"I'd rather be walking with you," she said, trying to sound confident.
