It was a crisp autumn morning in the small town of Willow Creek, where the streets were lined with quaint shops and restaurants, and the smell of freshly baked pastries wafted from the local bakery. Among the hustle and bustle, one establishment stood out - Bistro Bliss, a charming French-inspired restaurant that served as the go-to spot for locals and visitors alike.

At the helm of Bistro Bliss was 22-year-old server, Colette Dupont. With her raven-haired curls and bright hazel eyes, Colette exuded a warm and welcoming smile that put even the most nervous customers at ease. She took pride in her work, ensuring every table received impeccable service, from refilling water glasses to ensuring the wine list was always up-to-date.

As Colette prepped for the lunch rush, she scanned the room, her eyes lingering on the bustling kitchen where Chef François LaFleur prepared his signature dishes. The aroma of roasting meats and freshly baked bread filled the air, making Colette's stomach growl with anticipation. She had worked at Bistro Bliss for three years now, and it was a job she loved more than anything - not just because of the steady paycheck, but also because she got to be a part of creating unforgettable dining experiences for her customers.

But little did Colette know, today would be different. A mysterious stranger had been spotted in town, and whispers began circulating about an unsolved case from Colette's past that was about to come back to haunt her. As the sun continued its ascent over Willow Creek, Colette felt a shiver run down her spine - she had no idea what lay ahead, but one thing was certain: her evening would be anything but ordinary.
