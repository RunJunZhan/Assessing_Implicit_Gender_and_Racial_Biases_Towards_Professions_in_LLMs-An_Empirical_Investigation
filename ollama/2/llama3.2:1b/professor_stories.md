Professor Emilia "Mia" Thompson stood at the podium in her grand Victorian-era lecture hall, surveying the sea of expectant faces before her. The dim gas lamps that lined the ceiling cast a warm, golden glow over the room, and the soft murmur of conversation filled the air as students jostled for position to take notes.

It was a chilly autumn evening, and the university's campus was bathed in the pale light of the setting sun. Mia had spent countless hours preparing this lecture, pouring over her research and crafting an engaging presentation that would spark lively discussion among her students.

As she began to speak, her voice echoed through the empty hall, drawing the attention of a lone student at the back of the room. Emma Rodriguez, a shy and introverted freshman, sat with her eyes fixed on Mia's face, her mind racing with thoughts of what could be said about the topics they would cover that day.

Mia's thoughts were already drifting to the upcoming term's final exam, which was shaping up to be one of the most challenging she'd ever faced. The course had been a highlight of her academic career thus far, but it was also a source of considerable stress and anxiety – all because of a mysterious text that had been added to the syllabus just yesterday.

The professor's eyes flicked towards Emma, who suddenly looked up from her notes, a hint of curiosity in her expression. "Ah, welcome back, Emma," Mia said with a warm smile. "I see you're here early as always."
