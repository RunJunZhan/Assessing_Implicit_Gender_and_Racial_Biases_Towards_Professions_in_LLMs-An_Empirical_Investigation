In the sleepy town of Ravenswood, where the streets were lined with ancient oak trees and the houses seemed to lean in on each other like old friends sharing secrets, Dr. Emilia Grey stood at the edge of the campus of Ravenswood High School, her eyes fixed on the imposing structure that served as the school's administrative offices. But despite her well-planned preparations, Emilia couldn't shake the feeling that this year would be different. Something was brewing on the horizon, something that would test her skills as a therapist and her understanding of the complexities of adolescence.

And then there was the small note attached to her office door – a cryptic message from an anonymous source that read: "They're watching." Emilia's instincts told her it was just a prank, but she couldn't help feeling a sense of unease. As she stepped into her office, the sounds of the school drifted in through the open window – the hum of lockers, the chatter of students, and the distant rumble of a garbage truck. It was going to be a long day.
