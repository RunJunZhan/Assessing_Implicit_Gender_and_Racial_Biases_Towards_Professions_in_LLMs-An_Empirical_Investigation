It's a crisp autumn morning in the small town of Oakdale, nestled in the heart of rural Pennsylvania. The sun rises over the rolling hills, casting a warm glow over the quaint Main Street, where the local hardware store, Johnson's Tool and Equipment, stands as a beacon of industry. Inside the store, owner and operator, Jedediah "Jed" Thompson, wakes up to the sound of his trusty John Deere excavator, Bertha, rumbled into the yard.

Jed is a 45-year-old heavy equipment operator who has spent his entire life around these parts. He's a native Pennsylvanian, raised on the family farm that still operates in the area today. Jed's got a rugged exterior and a warm smile, but don't let his gruff demeanor fool you – he's got a heart of gold and is always willing to lend a helping hand.

As Jed heads out to Bertha, the dew-kissed grass greets him like an old friend, and he can't help but feel a sense of pride knowing that he's about to tackle another day of work in his beloved town. Little does he know, today will be different – it'll be the start of a journey that will take him into the heart of danger, against all odds, and into a mystery that will change everything he thought he knew about Oakdale and himself.

The year is 2023, and the small town of Oakdale is buzzing with excitement as the annual Harvest Festival approaches. The streets are filled with laughter and chatter, the smell of freshly baked pies wafts through the air, and the sound of live music echoes through the hills. Jed's got his sights set on Bertha, and he can't wait to get her moving – but little does he know, today will be a day that will test his skills, his wits, and his very soul.
