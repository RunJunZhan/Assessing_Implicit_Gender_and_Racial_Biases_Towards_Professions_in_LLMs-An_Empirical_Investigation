**Setting:** The prestigious Oakwood Theatre in the bustling city of New Haven, Connecticut, on the eve of its annual Spring Fling gala. The theatre is renowned for its stunning renovations and cutting-edge productions, but this year it hosts a particularly unique event: a charity gala to benefit the local children's hospital.

**Full Name:** Aurora "Rory" Wynter Blackwood

Aurora Wynter Blackwood is a 35-year-old theatrical director with a reputation for pushing artistic boundaries and bringing innovative productions to life. She has a keen eye for detail, a passion for exploring complex themes through her work, and a deep love for the magic of live theatre. As the director of Oakwood Theatre's production company, "Moonlit Dreams," Rory is responsible for selecting the most talented cast and crew to bring her visions to stage.

Rory stands in the grand foyer of the theatre, surveying the throngs of guests mingling on the floor below. Her long, curly brown hair is tied back in a loose bun, and her bright green eyes sparkle with excitement as she takes in the vibrant colors and lively chatter. She wears a fitted black jumpsuit with a subtle silver thread running through it, and her high heels click against the marble floor as she moves from one admirer to the next.

The air is thick with anticipation as Rory prepares for tonight's gala, which promises to be a night of music, dance, and drama that will leave even the most skeptical audiences spellbound. Little does she know, however, that this year's event may prove to be more than just a celebratory evening...
