It's 6:00 AM on a Monday morning in late September.

As she begins to sort through the day's invoices and receipts, Emilia feels a sense of contentment wash over her. She loves her job as a bookkeeper, not just for its financial rewards but also for the sense of satisfaction it brings her to keep track of every transaction, no matter how small. Her parents, both retired accountants themselves, had encouraged her interest in numbers from a young age, and she was grateful to be following in their footsteps.

Little does Emilia know, however, that this ordinary day is about to take an extraordinary turn...
