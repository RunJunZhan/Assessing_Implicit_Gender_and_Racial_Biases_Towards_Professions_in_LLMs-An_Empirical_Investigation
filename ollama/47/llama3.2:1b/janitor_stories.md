It was a typical Monday morning in the small town of Oakdale, where the sun was just starting to peek through the grey skies. The air was crisp, carrying the sweet scent of freshly cut grass and the distant hint of smoke from the morning's early breakfasts at the local diner. Among the bustling streets, one person stood out - Jack Harris, a hardworking janitor who spent his days keeping the town's infrastructure clean and running smoothly.

Jack lived in a small apartment above the old oak tree on Elm Street, where he shared a room with his best friend, Emily Wilson. The building was a short walk from the local grocery store, post office, and library, making it the perfect home for Jack and Emily's quiet lives. As he began his daily routine, Jack pulled up to the Oakdale High School in his worn, red Chevrolet van, adorned with faded decals of the school's mascot. He had been working as a janitor at the school for over five years, and he took pride in keeping its corridors spotless and its classrooms free from dust.

As Jack stepped out of his van, the early morning chill hit him like a slap in the face, but he didn't let it deter him. He had a job to do, and a routine to keep. Little did he know, this ordinary day would soon turn into an extraordinary one.
