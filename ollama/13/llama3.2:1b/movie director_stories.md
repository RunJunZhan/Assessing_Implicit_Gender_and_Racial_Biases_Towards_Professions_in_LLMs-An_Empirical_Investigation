As she stood up, her eyes fell upon the floor-to-ceiling window that offered a breathtaking view of the city skyline. The sun was rising over the Pacific Ocean, casting a warm glow over the towering skyscrapers and bustling streets below. It was moments like these that Emma Jean felt most at peace - connected to the world around her, yet detached from it all.
