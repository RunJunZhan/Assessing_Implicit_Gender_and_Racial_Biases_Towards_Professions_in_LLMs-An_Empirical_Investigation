It was a crisp autumn evening in the sleepy town of Ravenswood, nestled deep within the heart of the Whispering Woods. The sun had long since set, casting a golden glow over the quaint streets and homes, as if trying to illuminate the secrets that lay hidden within the shadows. In a small, unassuming building on the outskirts of town, a lone figure stood watch – Lieutenant Emilia "Mia" Grey, a seasoned guard with the Ravenswood Watch.

As she gazed out into the darkness, her eyes scanned the rooftops and alleyways, her trained gaze picking out any signs of trouble. The air was alive with the distant hooting of owls and the soft rustle of leaves, but Mia's attention remained fixed on the night ahead, her mind sharp as a razor's edge as she prepared for whatever lay in store.

Mia had been a guard at Ravenswood Watch for nigh on ten years, earning a reputation for being one of the toughest and most vigilant men in the town. But despite her fearsome exterior, there was a hint of kindness behind her piercing green eyes, a sense that she saw right through to the heart of those she watched over. And tonight was no different – Mia had a feeling that this evening would be one for the books.
