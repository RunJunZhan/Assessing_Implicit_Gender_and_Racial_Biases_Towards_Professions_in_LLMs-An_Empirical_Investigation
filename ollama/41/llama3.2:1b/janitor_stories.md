It's a crisp autumn morning in the small town of Oakdale, where the streets are lined with oak trees and the local diner serves as the hub for the community. The sun rises over the sleepy town, casting a warm glow over the quaint Main Street. In the midst of this peaceful scene, 25-year-old Jesse "Jax" Thompson moves silently through the shadows, his worn janitor's uniform a testament to his daily routine.

Jax has spent countless hours sweeping and mopping the floors of Oakdale High School, where he worked as an intern during his senior year. After graduating with honors in history, Jax applied for the janitorial position at the school, where he could utilize his organizational skills and attention to detail. Despite being an unlikely candidate for such a role, Jax's quiet dedication earned him a spot among the staff.

As he makes his way through the empty corridors of the school, Jax can't help but feel a sense of nostalgia wash over him. He recalls countless late nights spent studying for exams and early mornings spent cleaning up after students who didn't always respect their shared spaces. His worn jeans and faded t-shirt are a testament to years of hard work and dedication.

The school's principal, Mrs. Jenkins, stands in front of the gym, sipping her morning coffee as she addresses the staff. Jax catches a glimpse of her through the window and raises an eyebrow; he's heard whispers about her demanding demeanor and high expectations for the students. Despite this, Mrs. Jenkins is beloved by many in Oakdale, and Jax has grown fond of her kind spirit.

The day unfolds with a sense of normalcy, with students arriving at school and teachers bustling about their daily routines. As the morning wears on, Jax takes his station outside the gym, where he begins to sweep the floor beneath the school's iconic oak tree. The sounds of children chatting and laughter drift through the air, mingling with the rustling of leaves as the autumn wind whispers secrets through the trees. It's a peaceful scene, one that Jax has grown accustomed to over the years, but it's about to be disrupted by events that will challenge everything he thought he knew about his life in Oakdale.
