Here's a potential story opening and setting:

It was a drizzly autumn morning in late November when Dr. Evelyn Stone arrived at the dimly lit office of Ravenswood County Jail, her long, dark hair pulled back into a tight ponytail and her eyes scanning the deserted parking lot for any signs of trouble. As a forensic psychologist specializing in the mental health needs of prisoners, Evelyn had worked with the jail's inmates on numerous occasions, but this particular case had piqued her interest.

Rumors swirled that Ravenswood County Jail was home to one of the most notorious gangs in the country: the Red Vipers. Known for their brutal tactics and merciless violence, the Red Vipers had a stranglehold on the city's underworld, and Evelyn had been called in to help put an end to their reign.

Evelyn stepped into her office, a small, windowless room with stone walls that seemed to absorb the light around them. The only sound was the faint hum of a computer and the soft rustle of papers as she began to sort through the day's files.

As she pulled up a chair to her desk, Evelyn noticed a piece of paper on the floor that had been knocked over by one of the guards earlier that morning. She picked it up, smoothing out the creased paper to reveal a crude drawing of the Red Vipers' logo, with a single word scrawled in red ink: "WAIT."

Evelyn's eyes narrowed as she read the message. It seemed that someone was waiting for her inside the jail, and they were eager to discuss their next move...
