Here's a potential story opening and setting for a physical therapy drama:

**Setting:** The small town of Willow Creek, nestled in the heart of rural Ohio, where the lush green hills give way to dense forests and winding streams. It's the summer of 2005, and the air is filled with the sweet scent of blooming wildflowers and the distant chirping of birds.

**Main Character:**

Meet Dr. Emily Mayfield, a bright-eyed and ambitious physical therapist in her late 20s who has just moved to Willow Creek from the city. Emily's passion for helping others drive her to pursue a career in physical therapy, and she's eager to start her new job at the local hospital.

**Opening:**

As Emily steps out of her car and onto the cracked asphalt of Main Street, she takes a deep breath, feeling the warm sun on her skin and the gentle breeze rustling her hair. She can already smell the sweet aroma of freshly baked bread wafting from the nearby bakery and the savory scent of burgers sizzling on the grill at the local diner. This is it – she's officially started her new life in Willow Creek, and she can barely contain her excitement. But as she looks around at the quaint Main Street, she can't shake off the feeling that this small town may hold secrets and surprises that will test her skills as a physical therapist.

Emily's eyes land on the sleek, modern building across the street, home to the local hospital where she'll be working as a physical therapist. She feels a flutter in her chest at the thought of meeting her new colleagues and patients, but also a twinge of nerves – after all, she's not sure if she's truly ready for this life. As she begins to make her way towards the hospital, Emily can't help but wonder what the future holds for her in this charming, yet mysterious town.
