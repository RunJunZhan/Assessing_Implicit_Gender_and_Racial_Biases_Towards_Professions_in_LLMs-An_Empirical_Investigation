Sophia's office was just a stone's throw away, its windows glowing warmly from within like two green lanterns. It was a place where the boundaries between reality and fantasy blurred, where the complexities of human psychology were laid bare for all to see. As she stood there, lost in thought, Sophia couldn't help but feel a sense of nostalgia wash over her – a sense that this was where she belonged.
