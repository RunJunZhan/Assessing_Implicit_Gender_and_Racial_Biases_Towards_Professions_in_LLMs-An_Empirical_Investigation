Here's a potential story opening and setting for an Emergency Medical Services (EMS) protagonist:

---

The year was 2035, and the city of New Eden had seen better days. Once a thriving metropolis, it now struggled to balance growth with preservation. The streets were still lined with historic buildings, but they stood as a testament to a bygone era. The skyscrapers that pierced the sky seemed to lean in, as if sharing a secret, while the older structures whispered tales of a time long past.

Ava Moreno, codename "Raven," was a 28-year-old paramedic with the New Eden Emergency Medical Services (EMS). She worked the graveyard shift, patrolling the city's streets under the cover of darkness. Raven's eyes gleamed in the dim light as she navigated her silver Honda Civic through the deserted roads.

Raven lived and breathed EMS, but the job took a toll on her mental health. The long hours, the trauma, the constant exposure to suffering – it all began to wear down her armor. She had seen it all before: broken hearts, shattered lives, and the desperate prayers that echoed in her ears as she fought for a chance at salvation.

As she turned onto her street, Raven's gaze fell upon the familiar sight of her apartment complex, the one building that stood out from the rest – the high-rise with the crumbling facade. That was where she lived with her best friend and fellow paramedic, Carlos "Hawk" Hernandez. They shared a small studio on the top floor, overlooking the city below.

Raven's thoughts were interrupted by the beeping of her comms device. She answered it in a low tone, the words spilling out like a prayer. "Medic-12, this is Raven. What's your status?"

The voice on the other end was grim. "Raven, we've got multiple codes at 12 West and 14 North. Multiple patients, ranging from traumatic injuries to respiratory distress."

Raven's gut tightened as she hit play, her eyes locked onto the streets outside. This was what she signed up for – the unpredictability of the job, the weight of responsibility, and the unyielding human spirit that refused to give up.

"Roger that, Medic-12," Raven replied, even though she knew Carlos would be on his way soon. "Let's gear up and get this show on the road."

Little did she know, tonight was going to be a long, dark night – one that would test her limits, push her to the edge, and force her to confront the demons she'd been trying to keep at bay...
