It was a chilly autumn evening in the sleepy town of Ravenswood, where the streets were lined with old oak trees and the houses seemed to lean in, as if sharing secrets. The sun had long since set, casting a golden glow over the town, but a faint mist still lingered, shrouding the buildings in a damp, eerie silence.

In the heart of Ravenswood's downtown area, a lone figure stood at the entrance of City Watch Tower 7 - no one knew by his real name, only his codename, "Nightshade." He was a guard, tasked with protecting the town from any perceived threats that might arise. His eyes scanned the horizon, ever vigilant for any signs of trouble.

Nightshade had been working at City Watch for five years now, and in that time, he had grown accustomed to the rhythms of the town's life. He knew every alleyway, every street corner, and every hidden spot that only a native would know. But despite his familiarity with Ravenswood, he never took his job seriously enough.

Nightshade's uniform was a deep, dark blue, adorned with intricate silver stripes that shone like stars in the fading light. His eyes gleamed with a quiet intensity behind his sunglasses, and his shoulders were squared, ready to take on whatever challenges came his way. He was a man who preferred solitude to social interactions, and his nights spent patrolling the streets were the only times he felt truly alive.

As he stood watch, Nightshade's mind began to wander back to the events of that fateful night five years ago - the one that had changed him forever...
