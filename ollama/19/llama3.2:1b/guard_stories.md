It's a chilly autumn evening in the small town of Ravenswood, nestled deep within the heart of the Whispering Woods. The sun has long since set, casting a golden glow over the quaint streets and homes, but a soft, blue-green light seems to linger just beyond the edge of perception. This is the kind of eerie atmosphere that can make even the most seasoned guard, Elianore Quasar, feel like he's being watched.

Elianore stands at the entrance of Ravenswood's town hall, a sturdy stone building with towering spires and intricate carvings that seem to dance across its facade. He's been working as a guard here for nigh on five years, ever since completing his training at the prestigious Warden's Guard Academy. Elianore is just shy of thirty, with unruly brown hair and piercing green eyes that seem to bore into those he meets. He wears a crisp, black uniform adorned with the symbol of the Ravenswood town council – a silver eagle on a midnight blue background.

As he surveys his surroundings, Elianore notices a sense of unease settling over the town. The streets are empty, save for a few stragglers hurrying home from their daily chores or seeking shelter from the night. A faint scent of smoke lingers in the air, but it's not from any known fire. Instead, it smells like... something else entirely.
