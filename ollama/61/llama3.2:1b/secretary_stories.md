It was a typical Monday morning at Oakwood Towers, the prestigious corporate high-rise that dominated the city skyline. The sun had barely risen over the towering skyscraper, casting a golden glow over the bustling streets below. Inside the office, Emily Wilson, a soft-spoken and meticulous secretary, sipped her coffee as she began to prepare for another day of dealing with demanding clients and crunching numbers.

As she smoothed out her crisp white blouse and tied her hair back in a neat ponytail, Emily's thoughts wandered to the evening's schedule. She had promised her husband, John, that she would meet him at the art gallery opening at six, but a surprise rainstorm was forecasted for later that afternoon. She wasn't sure if it would be possible to make it out.

Emily's office was a cozy haven in the heart of Oakwood Towers, filled with the scent of fresh paper and the gentle hum of the fluorescent lights overhead. The walls were adorned with framed photographs of her family and friends, giving off an air of warmth and familiarity. As she made her way to the copier room, Emily couldn't help but feel a sense of contentment wash over her. This was her happy place – where the only deadlines were those imposed by her own imagination, and the only stress was the pressure to keep everything running smoothly.

As she locked the door behind her and began to file away any last-minute reports, Emily's gaze fell upon a small piece of paper on her desk with a cryptic message scrawled in red ink: "Meet me at the rooftop at midnight. Come alone." The words sent a shiver down her spine – what could this mysterious message possibly mean?
