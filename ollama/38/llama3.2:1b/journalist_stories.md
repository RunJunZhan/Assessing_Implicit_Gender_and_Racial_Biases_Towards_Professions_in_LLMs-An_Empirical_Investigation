As Lexi stepped out of her small apartment above the newspaper office, she felt a sense of excitement and trepidation wash over her. Today was the day she'd be heading out into the field with a promising lead on a story about a local businessman accused of embezzlement. The rain seemed to match the mood in her chest – dark and foreboding.

Lexi took a deep breath, shouldering her large backpack and slipping on her windbreaker as she set off into the misty morning air, ready to face whatever lay ahead.
