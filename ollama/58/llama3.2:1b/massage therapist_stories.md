The sun had just begun to set over the small coastal town of Ravenswood, casting a warm orange glow over the quaint streets and shops that lined the waterfront. The air was filled with the sweet scent of blooming lilacs and the sound of seagulls crying overhead as they prepared to take flight.

In a cozy little cottage on Elm Street, Emily Mayfield stirred in her bed, the soft morning light peeking through the curtains above her window. She stretched out her arms, feeling the gentle ache in her muscles after a long day of work at the local spa, where she specialized in massage therapy. As a licensed therapist, Emily had spent years honing her craft and building a loyal client base of clients seeking relaxation and relief from the stresses of everyday life.

As she swung her legs over the side of the bed, Emily's gaze fell upon the small framed photo on her nightstand - a picture of herself as a child, standing proudly beside her parents in front of their family's old beachside cottage. She smiled, remembering the countless afternoons spent playing in the sand and watching the waves roll in.

But despite the peacefulness of her home life, Emily couldn't shake off the feeling that something was amiss. A sense of restlessness had been building inside her for weeks now, a nagging feeling that she was meant for more than just helping others relax. She felt a sudden urge to break free from the routine and pursue a new dream - one that would take her away from the familiar rhythms of Ravenswood and into the unknown.

As Emily sat up in bed and began to get ready for the day, her mind wandered to a mysterious phone call she had received just the night before. The voice on the other end had been low and gravelly, speaking in hushed tones about something called "The Reckoning" - a secret event that would be taking place at an abandoned lighthouse on the outskirts of town, deep in the heart of Ravenswood's woods.

Little did Emily know, her ordinary life was about to take a dramatic turn.
