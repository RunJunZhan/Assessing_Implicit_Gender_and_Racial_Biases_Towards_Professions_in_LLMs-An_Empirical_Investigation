Here's a potential story opening and setting for an Emergency Medical Technician (EMT) novel:

---

The sun had barely risen over the small town of Oakdale, casting a warm glow over the quiet streets. But in the midst of this peaceful morning, a sense of unease settled over 25-year-old EMT, Alexandra "Lexi" Thompson. It was a feeling she'd grown accustomed to in recent months, ever since the sudden and unexpected death of her best friend and fellow officer, Emily Wilson.

Lexi had been part of the Oakdale EMS team for three years, earning the respect and admiration of her colleagues with her calm and compassionate demeanor. But despite her skills and experience, Lexi couldn't shake off the feeling that something was off. She'd noticed a change in the way the townspeople seemed to react to her, a mixture of curiosity and suspicion that made her feel like an outsider.

As she pulled up to the Oakdale Fire Department's station, Lexi's mind began to wander back to the night of Emily's death. The investigation had revealed nothing out of the ordinary, just a tragic accident involving a car crash on the rural roads outside town. But Lexi knew that was just what the department wanted to tell her – that everything would be okay, that it was all just a terrible coincidence.

As she stepped off her ambulance and onto the fire station's porch, Lexi felt a sense of familiarity wash over her. This was where Emily had spent countless hours before they'd gone their separate ways, working together to save lives on call. The familiar scent of fresh paint and sawdust filled her nostrils as Lexi gazed out at the quiet streets, wondering what lay ahead.
