Here's a potential story opening and setting for a novel about a dietician:

---

The sun had barely cracked the horizon over the rolling hills of Tuscany when Dr. Sophia Ellis stepped out of her family's old stone farmhouse and onto the dewy grass. She took a deep breath, feeling the warm Italian air fill her lungs as she gazed out at the breathtaking view before her. The vineyards stretched out in every direction, their terracotta-tiled roofs glinting like jewels in the morning light.

Sophia lived for days like this, when the world seemed to slow down and all that mattered was the simple pleasure of being alive. As a dietician specializing in healthy eating, she had always been passionate about helping people take control of their health through food. But now, at 35, Sophia felt restless – the same familiar routine as her family's farm, where her parents ran the local trattoria, was starting to feel stale.

As she walked back towards the farmhouse, Sophia couldn't shake the feeling that there was more to life than the quiet routine of farm work and family gatherings. She had always dreamed of leaving it all behind – starting her own practice, perhaps, or traveling the world as a food writer. But for now, she was content to focus on helping others find their own path.

Just then, Sophia's phone buzzed with an incoming text from her best friend and business partner, Rachel: "Hey girl! I just got back from a meeting in Florence – what about you? Did you hear anything interesting?"
