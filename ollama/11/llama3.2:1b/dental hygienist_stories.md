Here's a potential story opening for a mystery novel about a dental hygienist:

The misty morning air clung to the small town of Oakdale like a damp shroud, its residents still slumbering in their cozy beds after another lazy summer weekend. But for Dr. Emily Mayfield, a dental hygienist at the local Oakdale Dental Clinic, the fog was more than just a nuisance - it was a harbinger of doom.

Emily, a 32-year-old transplant to Oakdale from the city, had taken a job at the clinic after completing her dental hygiene program and landing an internship with the renowned Dr. Rachel Kim. The clinic's owner and founder, Dr. Kim had mentored Emily during her studies, and she had grown close to the warm and wise dentist.

As Emily arrived at the clinic, she was greeted by the sweet scent of freshly brewed coffee wafting from the kitchen below. She joined her colleague, Maria Rodriguez, a seasoned hygienist with years of experience under her belt, in the treatment room. Together, they prepped for their first appointment of the day, the soft hum of conversation and clinking instruments filling the air.

Just as Emily was about to begin her rounds, she noticed something off - Maria seemed... distracted. Her usual bright smile had faltered, and her eyes darted nervously towards the door. "What's wrong?" Emily asked, raising an eyebrow. Little did she know, this ordinary morning would soon become a catalyst for a mystery that would shake the very foundations of Oakdale Dental Clinic.
