She knew every inch of this waterway like the back of her hand, from the hidden shoals to the secret coves that only a handful of locals knew about.

As she took a deep breath of the salty air, Emily felt a sense of pride and satisfaction wash over her. This was her passion, her calling - sharing the beauty and magic of Coveville with visitors from all over the world. And she was grateful for the opportunity to do so.
