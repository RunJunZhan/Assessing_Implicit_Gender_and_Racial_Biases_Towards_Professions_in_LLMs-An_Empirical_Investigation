Here's a potential story opening and setting for a firefighter drama:

**Story Title:** "Ember"

**Main Character:** Alexandra "Lexi" Thompson, a 28-year-old seasoned firefighter with the City of New Haven Fire Department.

**Setting:** The year is 2023, and Lexi lives and works in the bustling coastal city of New Haven, Massachusetts. The city's picturesque coastline has been ravaged by a series of devastating hurricanes in recent years, leaving behind a trail of destruction and devastation. The streets are still recovering from the storms, but that's also when Lexi begins her new role as a firefighter at Station 17.

**Opening:**

The sun was setting over New Haven, casting a warm orange glow over the city's historic district. Lexi Thompson stood outside the fire station, sipping on a cup of coffee and checking the day's schedule one last time. As she gazed out at the ocean, she felt a sense of pride and purpose wash over her. It was going to be another long day, but she was ready for it. She had been a firefighter for five years now, and every call was a reminder that she was exactly where she wanted to be - in the midst of chaos, fighting fires, and making a difference.

Lexi's thoughts were interrupted by her team's chatter as they began to make their way towards Station 17. "Alright, Lexi, we've got a report of a possible structure fire at 345 Main St," called out her captain, Tom Bradley. Lexi nodded and followed the team into the station, her eyes scanning the area for any signs of trouble.

As she entered the firehouse, Lexi was greeted by the familiar sight of her teammates - Jake, Mike, and Emily - all sipping coffee and swapping stories about their day. She made her way over to them, dropping into a seat next to Jake. "Hey, guys," she said, handing him a cup of coffee. "Looks like we've got our first call of the day."
