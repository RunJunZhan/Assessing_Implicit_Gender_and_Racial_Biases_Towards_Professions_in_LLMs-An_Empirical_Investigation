But it also meant that the clinic would be quiet for months on end, leaving Mia feeling a little restless.

She had always dreamed of having her own veterinary practice, but after completing her residency at a top-tier animal hospital in New York City, she found herself back in Willow Creek, working alongside her parents and handling the day-to-day operations of their beloved clinic. Despite the familiarity and comfort it provided, Mia couldn't shake the feeling that there was more out there for her, waiting to be discovered.

As she finished up her tasks and locked up the clinic, Mia felt a spark of excitement ignite within her. She knew that this winter would bring its own unique challenges, but with them came opportunities to prove herself, to show the people of Willow Creek what she could do, and to follow her heart in the most unexpected ways.
