It was a crisp autumn morning in the small coastal town of Tidewater, Virginia, where the historic pier jutted out into the Atlantic Ocean like a skeletal finger. The air was alive with the sweet scent of saltwater and the distant chirping of seagulls, and the warm sunlight danced across the waves, casting a golden glow over the quaint shops and restaurants that lined the main street.

Aurora "Rory" Wellesley stood behind the counter of her family's beloved tour guide service, Wellesley Expeditions, a business she had run with her parents for generations. Rory was a third-generation tour guide, and as the current head of the operation, she took pride in knowing every nook and cranny of Tidewater like the back of her hand. She had spent countless hours studying the town's history, geology, and marine life, and it showed – Rory knew just the right words to say to entice passengers into exploring the local attractions.

As she polished a brass doorknob with a dirty rag, Rory's mind began to wander to the upcoming week's schedule. She had already finalized the itinerary for her popular "Tidewater Treasure Hunt" tour, which would take groups of tourists on a thrilling journey through the town's hidden coves and secret spots. But little did they know, this season was going to be one for the books – Rory had received a cryptic message from an anonymous source hinting at a long-lost treasure hidden somewhere in Tidewater...
