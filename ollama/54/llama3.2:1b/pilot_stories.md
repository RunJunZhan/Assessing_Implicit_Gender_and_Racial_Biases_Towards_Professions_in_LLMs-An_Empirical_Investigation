Ava's flight had been scheduled for weeks, and she knew that the tight security protocols would be in full swing by the time she took off. As a top intelligence officer with the US Air Force's Special Operations Command, Ava had seen her fair share of danger, but nothing could have prepared her for the day she received intel on a potential terrorist plot threatening to disrupt the world's supply chains.

Ava's small plane, affectionately nicknamed "The Midnight Sky", was a sturdy trusty companion that had gotten her through countless flights over the years. Its engines purred smoothly as Ava checked the instruments and ran through the pre-flight checks, her mind already focused on the day ahead.

As she finished up the last of the checks, Ava felt a sense of excitement mixed with nerves - it was going to be another long, challenging flight.
