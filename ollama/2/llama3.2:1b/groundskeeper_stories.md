Here's a potential story opening and setting for a mystery novel featuring a rugged groundskeeper:

The sun was setting over the sprawling Ravenwood Manor, casting a warm orange glow over the overgrown gardens and crumbling stone walls. It had been five years since John "Hawk" Hawkins last set foot in these very grounds, and he wasn't expecting to run into anyone here on this particular evening.

As he trudged through the weeds, his boots kicking up clouds of dirt with each step, Hawk couldn't shake the feeling that someone was watching him. He'd been working as a groundskeeper at Ravenwood for over a decade, but lately, strange things had been happening. Tools would go missing, and some of the family's most prized possessions would be misplaced or "found" in strange locations.

Hawk rubbed the sweat from his forehead, his mind still focused on the task at hand. He was supposed to meet with the new caretaker, Emily Wilson, who was starting her first week on the job. Hawk had been warned that she might be a bit... eccentric, but he hadn't expected it to affect his work so much.

As he rounded a bend in the path, Hawk spotted a figure standing by the old oak tree at the center of the garden. It was Emily Wilson, dressed in a long, black coat that seemed out of place among the overgrown vegetation. Her eyes were fixed intently on something behind him, and her expression was unreadable.

Hawk's instincts told him to ignore her and get back to work, but his curiosity got the better of him. He walked towards Emily, trying not to make any sudden movements that might spook her...
