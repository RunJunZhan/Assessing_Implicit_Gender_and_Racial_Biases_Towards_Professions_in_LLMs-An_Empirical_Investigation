Here's a potential story opening and setting:

In the heart of New York City, where the bright lights of Times Square faded into the darkness of 33rd Street, Dr. Ava Moreno stood at the edge of her hospital, the towering skyscrapers looming above her like sentinels. As a leading surgeon in the prestigious Manhattan General Hospital, Ava had grown accustomed to the hum of machinery and the beep of monitors, but nothing could have prepared her for the unexpected arrival of a young woman with a mysterious past.

The year was 2025, and the world was abuzz with the latest advancements in medical technology. But amidst the gleaming steel and the glow of holographic displays, Ava's attention was focused on a lone figure standing outside the hospital's emergency room, her eyes fixed on a small, leather-bound suitcase nestled between two worn hospital pillows.

Ava's gaze narrowed as she watched the woman approach, her dark hair pulled back into a tight ponytail, revealing a constellation of freckles across the bridge of her nose. A faint scent of lavender wafted from her person, an unusual combination that Ava hadn't encountered before. As the woman drew closer, Ava could see the faint glint of a silver earring in her left ear, and a small, antique pocket watch perched on the edge of her breast pocket.

Ava's trained eyes picked up on the air of quiet desperation emanating from the young woman, as if she had been carrying this burden for what felt like an eternity. Without a word, Ava pushed open the emergency room door and stepped into the chaos of the hospital, her professional composure faltering in the face of this enigmatic newcomer.

"Can I help you?" Ava asked, her voice firm but wary, as she fell into step beside the woman at the hospital entrance.
