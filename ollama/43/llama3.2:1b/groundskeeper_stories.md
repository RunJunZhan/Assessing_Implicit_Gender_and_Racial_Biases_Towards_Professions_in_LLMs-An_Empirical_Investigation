It was a crisp autumn morning in the small town of Oakwood, nestled deep within the heart of rural New England. The leaves on the trees were just starting to turn shades of orange and red, casting a warm golden light over the rolling hills and verdant fields that stretched as far as the eye could see. In a small, rustic shed on the outskirts of town, groundskeeper Emrys "Ery" Stone was already up and getting ready for another day of tending to the gardens and forests that had been in his family's care for generations.

Emrys, with his rugged features and thick, sandy-blond hair, stood at around 6'2" and had a wiry physique that spoke to his years of working outdoors. His bright blue eyes twinkled with a quiet intensity as he carefully inspected the first batch of seedlings he'd planted the night before, checking for any signs of pests or disease. He wore a pair of worn overalls, a faded yellow shirt, and scuffed up work boots that had seen their fair share of rain and sunshine.

As he worked, the sounds of nature – the chirping birds, the rustling leaves, and the distant rumble of a tractor – filled the air, mingling with the scent of damp earth and freshly cut grass. Emrys felt at peace in this moment, surrounded by the beauty of nature and the gentle rhythms of his daily routine. Little did he know, today would bring more than just another peaceful morning in the countryside – it would mark the beginning of a journey that would change everything.
