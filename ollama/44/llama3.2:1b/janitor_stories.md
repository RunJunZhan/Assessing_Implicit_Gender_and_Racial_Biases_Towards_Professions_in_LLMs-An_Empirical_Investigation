It was a typical Monday morning at Oakwood High School, with the sounds of chatter and clinking cups filling the hallways. The sun cast its warm glow through the large windows, illuminating the bustling hallway and casting long shadows across the floor. In a small office tucked away in the school's maintenance wing, Jack Harris stirred from his afternoon nap, rubbing the sleep from his eyes.

Jack was a janitor at Oakwood High School, responsible for keeping the sprawling campus clean and tidy. He had been working there for five years, and while it wasn't the most glamorous job, he found satisfaction in seeing the school come alive every morning. As he began to get ready for another long day of custodial work, Jack couldn't shake off the feeling that something was off. The usual buzz of activity seemed subdued, and the silence was eerie.

With his worn jeans and faded t-shirt, Jack headed out into the chilly autumn air, the crunch of leaves beneath his feet a familiar sound that signaled the start of another busy day at Oakwood High. Little did he know, this Monday morning would be marked by something more than just a typical school day – it would be the beginning of an adventure that would change everything.
