She had built a loyal following among the local residents, who appreciated her warm bedside manner and commitment to providing exceptional care.

Her team of two assistants, Emma and Ryan, bustled about, preparing for the day's procedures. Dr. Patel took a deep breath, feeling a sense of calm wash over her as she prepared to face another busy day at Oakwood Dental Care. She had always dreamed of running her own practice, and now, at 35, that dream was finally within reach.
