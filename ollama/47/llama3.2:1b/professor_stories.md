Here's a potential story opening and setting:

Professor Aurelia "Rory" Thompson stood at the edge of her college campus, gazing out at the vast expanse of rolling hills and fields that stretched as far as the eye could see. She took a deep breath, feeling the crisp morning air fill her lungs, and began her walk to the small cottage on the outskirts of town where she lived with her grandmother.

The summer had arrived early this year, bringing with it a sense of languor and possibility that Rory had grown accustomed to over the years. As an assistant professor at Oakwood University, she had spent countless hours teaching and mentoring students, but in the past few weeks, she had found herself with more time on her hands than usual.

The cottage was cozy, with a fire crackling in the hearth and a garden bursting with colorful flowers that spilled out onto the porch. Rory loved this time of year, when the world felt fresh and full of promise. She had always been drawn to the idea of small-town life, where everyone knew each other's names and faces, and the air was thick with gossip and community spirit.

As she walked, Rory couldn't help but think about her upcoming summer vacation plans. She had a writing retreat in the nearby mountains, where she hoped to focus on her novel-in-progress and perhaps even finish the manuscript by fall semester. The prospect of some much-needed solitude and creative time was just what she needed after a long year of teaching and administrative duties.

But Rory's mind was also preoccupied with other things - the rumors circulating about a potential academic scandal at Oakwood, and the whispered conversations in hushed tones between faculty members who seemed to be waiting for her to drop something big. As a respected scholar in her field, she had always been mindful of the importance of maintaining a professional demeanor, but lately, she couldn't shake the feeling that something was off, just beneath the surface...
