It was a crisp autumn morning in the small town of Ravenswood, nestled deep within the English countryside. The leaves on the oak trees outside the town hall rustled gently in the early breeze, casting dappled shadows on the cobblestone streets. Within the walls of the town hall, a lone figure stood watch at the entrance to the grand staircase, his name etched into the stone above the door: Ethan Blackwood.

Ethan was a guard of great renown in Ravenswood, known for his unwavering dedication and unshakeable sense of justice. His piercing blue eyes scanned the streets below with a practiced intensity, ever vigilant for any sign of trouble or deception. As he stood there, lost in thought, the soft chime of a clock tower echoed through the town hall's great hall, marking the start of another day in this quiet, idyllic village.

The villagers, going about their daily routines, spoke little of Ethan's nocturnal patrols, preferring to leave his duties to those who wore them. But whispers began to circulate among the townsfolk – rumors of strange occurrences and unexplained happenings at night, which some attributed to Ethan's keen senses and sharp instincts as a guardian. Little did they know that on this particular morning, Ethan was about to stumble upon something that would change everything.
