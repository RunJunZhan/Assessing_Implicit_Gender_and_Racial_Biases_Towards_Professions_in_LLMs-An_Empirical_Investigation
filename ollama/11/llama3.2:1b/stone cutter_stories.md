The mist-shrouded mountains of Kaelin's Peak stretched out before Emrys Stonefist like a spectral landscape, his weathered face set in a resolute expression as he guided his worn leather wagon through the winding roads. As a stone cutter by trade, Emrys had spent countless hours within these hills, shaping and crafting the rocky masses into beautiful works of art that adorned the village below.

Born to a family of humble stone masons, Emrys had learned the trade from a young age, but he had always dreamed of creating something more. A talent for carving and chiseling had been nurtured in his childhood, and as he grew older, he found himself drawn to the ancient, worn stones that littered the mountainsides.

Now, at the ripe old age of 45, Emrys stood out among the other villagers, who saw him as just another rugged mountain man. But few knew the depth of his passion for shaping stone into works of beauty and artistry. And yet, he felt a constant sense of restlessness, a feeling that there was more to life than simply cutting stones all day.

Little did Emrys know, his quiet existence about to be disrupted by a mysterious stranger who would set him on a journey that would change the course of his life forever...
