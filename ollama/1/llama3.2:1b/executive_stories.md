Among them was Alexandra "Lexi" Thompson, a 35-year-old executive who sat confidently at her desk, sipping on a cup of coffee and scanning the newspaper as she waited for her assistant to arrive.

Lexi's full name was Lexie Anne Thompson, and she was the CEO of Oakwood Industries, a leading manufacturer of cutting-edge technology solutions. Her sleek black hair was tied back in a tight ponytail, revealing a sharp jawline and piercing green eyes that seemed to gleam with a quiet intensity as she contemplated the challenges ahead.

As the doors opened, Lexi's assistant, Emily, slipped into the lobby, a bright smile on her face. "Good morning, Mrs. Thompson! Your meeting with the board is in 30 minutes. Shall I brief you on any updates?"

Lexi nodded, her eyes never leaving the newspaper as she replied, "Yes, go ahead."
