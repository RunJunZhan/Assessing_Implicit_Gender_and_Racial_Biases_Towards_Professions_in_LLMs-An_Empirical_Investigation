Here's a potential story opening and setting for an Emergency Medical Technician (EMT) named Maya Blackwood:

It was a crisp autumn morning in the small town of Oakdale, nestled in the heart of rural Georgia. The leaves were just starting to change colors, casting a warm orange glow over the sleepy streets. But amidst the beauty of nature, a sense of unease settled over the residents as they went about their daily routines.

Maya Blackwood, her dark hair pulled back into a tight ponytail and her scrubs adorned with the Oakdale EMS logo, stood outside the local hospital, scanning the horizon for any sign of trouble. As an EMT with Oakdale EMS, Maya had seen it all - from frantic callers to mangled accidents and even the occasional heart attack. But today felt different. Today, there was a sense of anticipation, as if something big was waiting just beyond the town's borders.

Maya took a deep breath, shouldering her bag and heading into the hospital's emergency room. She had been on duty for only a few hours, but already she was surrounded by the sounds of beeping machines and muffled conversations. Her partner, Jake, was waiting for her in the ER, his eyes scanning the chaos with a look of practiced calm.

"What've you got?" Maya asked, falling into step beside him as they made their way through the crowded room.

"Just another caller," Jake replied, nodding towards the reception desk. "Should be here any minute."

Maya followed Jake's gaze to the waiting area, where a harried-looking nurse was fielding phone calls and typing away on her computer. She spotted a figure huddled in the corner, clad in a dark hoodie and sunglasses - someone who didn't seem to fit in with the rest of Oakdale's inhabitants.

"Hey, Maya," Jake said quietly, as if sharing a secret. "I think our visitor might be trouble."
