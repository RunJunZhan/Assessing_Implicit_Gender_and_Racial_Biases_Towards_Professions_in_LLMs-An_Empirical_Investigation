Here's a potential story opening and setting for you:

The rain-soaked streets of Ashwood City glistened like polished onyx as Jack "Mac" McCarthy swept the floor of the old municipal building with a loud swoop of his mop. A janitor by trade, Mac had seen it all from behind the worn steel doors: the whispered secrets in the staff rooms, the late-night revelries at the bars on Main Street, and the endless stream of visitors to the city's neglected institutions.

As he worked, the sound of raindrops pattering against the roof above grew louder, and Mac knew the storm was finally starting to clear. He finished up his sweep and leaned on the mop handle for a moment, surveying the familiar space one last time before shutting off the lights that would extinguish the building's fluorescent glow.

The city outside was quiet, the only sound the distant hum of the highway and the occasional chirp of a lonely bird. But Mac knew that with this kind of weather, Ashwood City was never truly asleep – not for long, anyway.
