It was a warm summer evening in New York City, with the sun setting over the towering skyscrapers of Manhattan as Flight Attendant Alexandra "Lexi" Thompson prepared for her shift at JFK Airport. She lived for this time of day, when the hustle and bustle of the airport gave way to a more relaxed atmosphere, and she could focus on her duties without the constant noise and chaos.

As she tied up her uniform and grabbed her bag, Lexi thought back to how she ended up in this role. After college, she had worked various jobs, but nothing seemed to stick until she landed an internship at United Airlines. She had been thrilled when offered a full-time position as a flight attendant, and it was a dream come true.

Now, five years later, Lexi loved every minute of her job. She took pride in providing excellent customer service, ensuring the safety of passengers, and making sure their flights ran on time. But despite her love for flying, she never thought she'd find herself as invested in people's lives as she was now – especially when it came to one special someone who was about to change everything.

Lexi had been assigned to Flight 121 from New York to Los Angeles, a direct route that would take her through the bustling streets of the city. As she boarded the plane and took her place at the front row, she couldn't help but feel a thrill of excitement and a hint of nervousness. Little did she know, this flight would be more than just a routine trip – it was one that would bring her face-to-face with someone who would challenge everything she thought she knew about love, friendship, and herself.
