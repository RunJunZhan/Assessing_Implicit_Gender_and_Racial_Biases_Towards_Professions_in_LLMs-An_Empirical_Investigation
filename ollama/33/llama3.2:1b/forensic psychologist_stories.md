It was a drizzly autumn evening in the small town of Ravenswood, nestled in the heart of New England's countryside. The streets were dimly lit, the only sound being the patter of raindrops on the pavement and the distant hum of headlights from the few cars that had ventured out into the darkness.

Dr. Emilia "Mia" Grey stood outside the local police station, her eyes fixed on the nondescript building across the street. As a forensic psychologist with the Ravenswood County Sheriff's Office, Mia had been called to the station several times in the past year due to some of the more...unusual cases that came through their doors.

Mia was a tall, slender woman in her late 30s with short, dark hair and piercing green eyes. She had a no-nonsense attitude and a sharp mind, but also a soft spot for those in need of help. Her rugged, weathered features were testament to the many long hours she spent studying the human mind.

As she waited for the chief to arrive, Mia's gaze drifted back to her notes and file folders that littered the steps leading up to the station door. She was reviewing a new case that had come in earlier that day - a young woman named Sarah Jenkins who had been accused of murdering her husband in their home. The police had found no signs of forced entry or struggle, but they did have some disturbing evidence.

Mia's mind began to wander back to the early stages of the investigation, when she had helped interview Sarah and analyzed the physical evidence. She remembered the way Sarah's eyes had seemed to lock onto hers during their first meeting, a fleeting glimmer of something that made Mia feel like she was looking into her own soul.

The door burst open, and Chief Jameson strode out into the dimly lit hallway, his rugged face illuminated by the faint glow of the security light overhead. "Mia, thanks for coming in on short notice," he said, his deep voice gruff but friendly. "I know you've been doing some research on Sarah Jenkins."

Mia nodded, tucking a stray strand of hair behind her ear as she followed Chief Jameson into the station's cramped waiting area. She spotted the chief's partner, Detective Rachel Lee, sitting in the corner with a cup of coffee and a notebook, her eyes fixed intently on the screen.

"Tell me more about Sarah," Mia said, taking a seat across from Chief Jameson.
