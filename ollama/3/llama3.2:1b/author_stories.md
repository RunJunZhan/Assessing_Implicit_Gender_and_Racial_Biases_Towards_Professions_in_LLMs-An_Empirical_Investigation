Here's a potential story opening and setting:

In the sleepy coastal town of Raven's Cove, nestled between the crashing waves of the Pacific Ocean and the rugged cliffs that met the sea, lived Alexandra "Lexi" Thompson - a renowned author of Gothic novels known for their atmospheric settings and mysterious characters. Lexi was a recluse, preferring to spend her days holed up in her cozy beachside cottage, surrounded by books and scribbled notes, rather than socializing with the people who inhabited the town.

The sun had barely risen over the horizon when Lexi stirred from her slumber, the sound of the waves and the cries of seagulls outside her window a welcome alarm. She stretched her arms above her head, yawned, and swung her legs over the side of the bed, her feet dangling in front of the fireplace that crackled and popped with warm embers. It was going to be another long day ahead.

As she made her way downstairs to the kitchen, the aroma of freshly brewed coffee wafted through the air, mingling with the scent of saltwater and sea spray. Lexi's eyes widened as she took in the sight before her - a steaming pot of coffee sat on the counter, alongside a plate of croissants and muffins that had been baked by her neighbor, Mrs. Jenkins.

It was going to be just another ordinary day in Raven's Cove, until suddenly...
