Her latest book, "The Shadow in the Valley," had been making waves in the literary world, but Emilia wasn't one to seek fanfare. She preferred to let her words speak for themselves, as she delved into her latest manuscript with a sense of quiet determination.

As she typed away on her old typewriter, a faint knock at the door broke the stillness of the night. It was just her housekeeper, Mrs. Jenkins, come to check on Emilia's progress and leave her some supper. But as Emilia turned back to the page, a sudden gust of wind blew open the window, sending a cold draft wafting into the room and extinguishing the candles.

The darkness seemed to close in around Emilia, and she felt a shiver run down her spine as she wondered if she was truly alone in Willow Creek.
