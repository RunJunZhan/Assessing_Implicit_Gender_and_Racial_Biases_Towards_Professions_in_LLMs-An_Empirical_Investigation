It's a chilly autumn morning in the small town of Ravenshire, nestled deep within the English countryside. The misty dawn air clings to the quaint streets and alleys, casting a mystical spell over the sleepy residents as they go about their daily routines. In the heart of this charming village, a lone figure emerges from the shadows of Ravenshire Castle, its stone walls towering above the town like a sentinel guarding centuries of secrets.

Meet Ethan Wychwood, a seasoned guard who has sworn to protect the castle and its inhabitants from the outside world. With his rugged good looks and piercing blue eyes, Ethan exudes an air of quiet authority, but beneath his stoic exterior lies a complex web of emotions and motivations that only he can decipher.

As Ethan makes his way through the castle's winding corridors, the soft chirping of birds and the distant hum of village life provide a soothing background melody to his thoughts. He has been on duty for what feels like an eternity, but tonight is different. Tonight, the castle's residents are gathered in the great hall, eager to celebrate the approaching harvest festival with feasting, music, and merriment. Ethan's task is to ensure that the festivities remain peaceful and prosperous, a responsibility he takes seriously.

As he approaches the great hall, Ethan notices a sense of unease simmering just below the surface among his fellow guards. It's not just the usual petty squabbles or routine threats from outside; there's something more at play, something that threatens to disrupt the carefully laid plans for the evening. Little does Ethan know, tonight will be the start of a long and winding road that will test his courage, his wits, and his unwavering dedication to the castle and its people.
