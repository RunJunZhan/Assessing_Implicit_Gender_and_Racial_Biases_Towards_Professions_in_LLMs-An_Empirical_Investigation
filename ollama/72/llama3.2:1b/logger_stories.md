The misty dawn crept over the dense forest like a living entity, shrouding everything in a damp, gray veil. Logger Ethan Blackwood stirred in his small cabin on the outskirts of Raven's Peak, the distant rumble of chainsaws and the murmur of hammers echoing through the air as he went about his morning routine.

Ethan had spent most of his life working in these woods, learning the trade from his father before him. He knew every inch of this forest, every hidden stream and every towering tree. But today was different; a thick fog had rolled in overnight, reducing visibility to near zero. The logging trucks wouldn't be able to get through until morning, and Ethan knew he'd have to spend the day at the campsite with his crew.

As he dressed in the chill of the early morning air, the creaking of the old cabin and the rustling of leaves outside were a stark reminder that this forest was full of secrets, and sometimes even its inhabitants.
