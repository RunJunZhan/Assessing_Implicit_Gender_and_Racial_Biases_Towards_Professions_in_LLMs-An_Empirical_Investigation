The misty dawn air clung to the small town of Ravenshire like a damp shroud, casting a mysterious veil over the quaint streets and crumbling buildings that lined the riverbank. In a world where stones were the lifeblood of the town, 25-year-old Kaelin Darkhaven was already up before the sun, his calloused hands gripping the worn wooden handle of his trowel as he began his daily ritual: carefully cutting and shaping the rough-hewn granite blocks that lined the riverfront. It was a solitary job, one that required patience, skill, and an eye for beauty - and Kaelin took pride in knowing that every stone he cut would eventually be transformed into something new, something unique.

As he worked, the sound of the river's gentle lapping against the shore provided a soothing background melody, accompanied by the occasional clang of metal on stone as Kaelin hammered out a rhythmic beat with his trowel. It was a peaceful beginning to another long day in Ravenshire, where the rhythms of nature and the pulse of the town's history governed every move. Little did he know, however, that today would bring more than just routine – it would bring a call that would set him on a path that would change everything.
