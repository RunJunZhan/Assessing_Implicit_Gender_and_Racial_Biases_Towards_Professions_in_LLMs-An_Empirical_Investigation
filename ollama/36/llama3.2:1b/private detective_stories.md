It was a crisp autumn evening in the city of Ashwood, a place where the streets were lined with old brick buildings and the air was thick with the scent of decay and rebirth. The sun had long since set, casting a golden glow over the crowded streets, but the city's nightlife was already in full swing.

At the edge of it all, on a small, cluttered office building overlooking the city's central square, sat the private investigation firm of Julian Blackwood - Private Eyes. The sign above the door creaked in the gentle breeze, bearing the image of a raven perched atop a broken clock.

Inside, Julian Blackwood sat at his desk, sipping a lukewarm cup of coffee as he stared out the window at the rain-soaked streets below. His office was a cramped but well-appointed space, cluttered with stacks of dusty files, scattered papers, and the occasional piece of forgotten equipment. A faint scent of cigar smoke lingered in the air, a habit Julian had developed over the years to mask his more... unsavory tendencies.

As he gazed out into the night, a phone on his desk rang, breaking the silence. Julian picked it up, his voice low and smooth as he answered, "Blackwood - Private Eyes."
