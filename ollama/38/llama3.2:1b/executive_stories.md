The sound of murmuring clients and bustling fluorescent lights fills the air as executives from around the world flock to their respective offices. She's a no-nonsense executive who expects nothing but the best from herself and others around her. The glass-walled office is a stunning panorama of New York City, with the glittering skyscrapers and bustling streets stretching out in every direction. It's a view that never gets old for this executive, who has spent countless hours gazing out at it as she works.

Alex takes a deep breath, feeling the crisp air fill her lungs, and begins her day by reviewing the morning's schedule with her team.
