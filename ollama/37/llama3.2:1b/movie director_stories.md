Here's a potential story opening and setting for a film about a movie director:

**Title:** "The Camera's Eye"

**Main Character:** Alexandra "Lexi" Thompson, a 35-year-old award-winning film director known for her visually stunning and emotionally charged movies.

**Story Setting:** The small coastal town of Tidewater, Oregon, where Lexi grew up surrounded by the rugged beauty of the Pacific Ocean and the dense forests that meet it. Tidewater has been home to Lexi since she was a child, and she still returns frequently to visit her family's old beachside cottage and revisit the places that shaped her childhood dreams.

Tidewater is a tight-knit community with a rich artistic heritage, where everyone knows each other's business and secrets are hard to keep. The town has a thriving film industry, with many local studios and production companies based there. Lexi's latest project, a drama series about a group of coastal fishermen struggling to adapt to the changing tides of their industry, is currently in development at one of the local studios.

As the summer solstice approaches, Tidewater is buzzing with excitement and nervous energy. The town is preparing for its annual Film Festival, which attracts visitors from all over the world who come to celebrate the art of filmmaking. Lexi's latest movie, a romantic drama about two strangers who fall in love under the stars, is set to premiere at the festival, but she's still feeling anxious and unsure if it will be a hit...
