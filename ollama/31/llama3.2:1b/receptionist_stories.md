It was a typical Monday morning at Oakwood Manor, a grand estate nestled deep in the English countryside. The sun rose over the manicured lawns, casting a warm glow over the sprawling grounds. Receptionist Emily Wilson wiped the sleep from her eyes and rubbed the tired muscles from her neck as she checked her phone for any urgent messages.

As she stood by the front desk, sipping on a lukewarm cup of coffee, she surveyed the bustling hallway. The morning light danced across the polished marble floor, highlighting the golden accents that adorned the portraits of past residents. Emily's eyes landed on a photograph tacked to the wall behind her desk – a group shot from her high school graduation party, with her family beaming in the center.

With a quiet hum of satisfaction, Emily began her daily routine, greeting each visitor with a warm smile and a practiced professional demeanor. As she took orders for the day's schedule, answered phone calls, and handled the occasional emergency, she felt a sense of comfort in her role as the gatekeeper to Oakwood Manor. Little did she know, today would bring more than just the usual comings and goings – it was about to get a whole lot stranger.
