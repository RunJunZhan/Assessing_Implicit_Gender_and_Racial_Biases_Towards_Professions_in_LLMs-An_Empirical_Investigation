It was a crisp autumn morning in the small town of Willow Creek, nestled in the heart of rural England. The year was 1955, and the air was alive with the sweet scent of ripe apples from the local orchard. In a cozy little cottage on Elm Street, Mrs. Emily Fanshawe, affectionately known as Em to her friends and family, stirred awake from her deep slumber. She lived alone, having lost her husband two years ago in a tragic accident, and took care of her ailing mother, who resided across the street.

As she dressed in the dim light of her bedroom window, Em's mind wandered to the day ahead. She had promised herself a few hours of gardening in the backyard before heading off to Willowdale Manor, the grand estate of the reclusive Mr. Edward Blackstone, where she worked as the housekeeper. The manor was a sprawling mansion with its own secrets and mysteries, and Em had grown fond of its eerie yet comforting atmosphere.

With her hair tied back in a neat bun and a warm smile on her face, Em set out into the morning mist to start her day's work, ready to tackle the tasks that lay before her. The sun was beginning to peek through the trees as she made her way down the narrow path to the garden shed, where the tools and equipment for her job were kept. Little did she know, today would bring more than just a typical gardening day...
