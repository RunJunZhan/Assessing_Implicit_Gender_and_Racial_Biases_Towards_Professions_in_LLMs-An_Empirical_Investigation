He rubbed the sleep from his eyes and swung his legs over the side of the bed, planting his feet firmly on the ground as he began to get ready for another long day ahead.

Ethan lived in a small studio apartment above a vintage clothing store, with a breathtaking view of the city skyline that had become a daily inspiration for his creativity. As he poured himself a cup of steaming hot coffee and grabbed his worn leather backpack, he couldn't help but feel a sense of excitement and purpose wash over him. Today was going to be one of those days where everything came together just right – maybe a successful project meeting at 10 am, followed by a lunch date with an old friend, and then perhaps even a side hustle opportunity to test his coding skills.

As he headed out the door, Ethan glanced down at the full name printed on his ID card: Ethan Wright. It was more than just his first name – it was a reminder of who he was, where he came from, and what drove him every day to create something new and innovative. Little did anyone know that today would be one of those days that would test Ethan's skills like never before...
