It was a crisp autumn morning in the charming coastal town of Tidal Cove, where the scent of saltwater and freshly baked croissants wafted through the air as 25-year-old interior decorator, Alexandra "Lexi" Thompson, stepped out of her sleek, silver Mercedes and onto the weathered wooden porch of her family's historic beachside bungalow. The warm golden light of dawn danced across the waves crashing against the shore, casting a serene glow over the sleepy town.

Lexi took a deep breath, savoring the invigorating air and the sound of seagulls crying overhead. She had always been drawn to Tidal Cove's laid-back vibe and natural beauty, which inspired her interior design firm, Thompson Interiors. With her signature blend of coastal chic and eclectic flair, Lexi had established a loyal client base in the area, helping homeowners like her family's friends and acquaintances create stunning homes that reflected their personal style.

As she pushed open the creaky door, a warm breeze swept inside, carrying with it the faint hint of lavender and lemon. Lexi smiled to herself, knowing exactly where she wanted to be – in her own cozy haven, surrounded by the comforting scent of fresh paint and the soothing sounds of the ocean. Little did she know, this day would bring more than just a chance to recharge; it was about to get a whole lot busier...
