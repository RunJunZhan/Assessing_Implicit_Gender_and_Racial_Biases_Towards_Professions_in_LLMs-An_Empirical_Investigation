It was a crisp autumn morning in the small town of Tahoma, nestled in the heart of the Pacific Northwest. The sky was a deep shade of indigo, with just a hint of pink tinting the horizon as the sun slowly rose above the mountains. The air was cool and damp, carrying the sweet scent of ripe cherries from the nearby orchards.

Aurélien "Rye" LaFleur stood on the edge of the small general store, his feet dangling over the precipice as he gazed out at the breathtaking view. He had just finished a pre-flight check of his Cessna 182 at the local airport, and was now taking in the peaceful morning atmosphere before heading back to the hangar for a final inspection.

Rye was a pilot with a reputation for being one of the most skilled and adventurous in the region. With over 5,000 hours of flight time under his belt, he had built a loyal following among the townspeople, who would often gather at the airport to watch him take off and land. His black hair was tied back in a ponytail, revealing a smattering of freckles across his cheeks, and his piercing blue eyes sparkled with a hint of mischief as he admired the view.

As he stood there, lost in thought, Rye's mind began to wander to the upcoming Airshow of Oakwood, where he would be competing against some of the best pilots from around the country. He had always loved the thrill of competition, and the opportunity to showcase his skills to a new audience.

Little did Rye know, today was going to be one of those days that would change everything.
