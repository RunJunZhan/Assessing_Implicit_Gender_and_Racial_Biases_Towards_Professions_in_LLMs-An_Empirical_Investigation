The misty veil of morning crept over the sleepy town of Ravenswood, shrouding its quaint streets in an impenetrable fog. Officer Emilia Grey stood at the edge of the diner, sipping her coffee and scanning the gloomy scene before her. The local police department's headquarters loomed behind her, a nondescript building with a faded sign creaking in the gentle breeze.

Ravenswood was a place where everyone knew each other's names and faces, where the air was sweet with the scent of freshly baked pies and where the only crime was the ones committed by the town's own residents. It was a community that prided itself on its values of kindness, cooperation, and small-town charm.

But amidst this tranquil facade, Emilia had received a cryptic message from her department's new internal affairs unit, requesting her presence at a discreet meeting in the dimly lit back room of the local sheriff's office. The tone of the message was laced with an air of urgency, and Emilia's instincts told her that something was amiss.

As she finished her coffee and prepared to leave, a gust of wind blew open the diner's door, revealing a fleeting glimpse of a young woman with piercing green eyes and raven-black hair, who looked like she had just stepped out of a rain-soaked nightmare.
