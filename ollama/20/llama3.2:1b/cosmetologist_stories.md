It was a crisp autumn morning in the small coastal town of Tidal Cove, where the smell of saltwater and freshly baked pastries wafted through the air. The sun was just starting to rise over the horizon, casting a warm glow over the quaint shops and restaurants that lined the main street. Among them was "Blossoming Beauty," the local beauty salon owned by 25-year-old hair stylist and cosmetologist, Aria Wells.

Aria woke up early, as she always did, before the rest of the town stirred. She stretched her toned arms and yawned, feeling the familiar creak of her bed frame as she rolled out of bed. After a quick shower and getting dressed in her signature bohemian style, Aria headed to the kitchen to make some breakfast for herself and her little brother, Jasper. Her mom had left a fresh pot of coffee brewing on the stovetop, along with a tray of pastries that she always saved for Aria's morning routine.

As she sat down at the table, sipping her coffee and nibbling on a flaky croissant, Aria gazed out the window at the sea of sailboats bobbing gently in the harbor. She loved this time of day, when the town was still quiet and everyone had a chance to enjoy the peaceful atmosphere before the hustle and bustle of the summer season began.

With her breakfast finished, Aria grabbed her hairbrush and headed out to tackle the morning's appointments at Blossoming Beauty. As she walked down the main street, she noticed a flyer on the community bulletin board outside the local library - an ad for a free makeup workshop hosted by the town's mayor, inviting all residents to learn more about sustainable beauty practices. Aria's eyes lit up with excitement as she pulled out her phone to save the date and start thinking about how she could share this information with her clients...
