The rain-soaked streets of New Haven were always at their most unforgiving on nights like these, but for 25-year-old electrician August "Augie" Thompson, they seemed to be having an unusual effect. The black ice that made the roads slick and treacherous was nothing compared to the dark energy brewing inside Augie's own mind.

Growing up in a family of modest means, Augie had always felt like there was something missing - a spark he couldn't quite ignite. It wasn't until his late teenage years, when his father passed away after a freak electrical accident, that Augie discovered his true calling: working with electricity to control the very forces that drove it.

Now, as he stood on the corner of Elm and Maple, fiddling with the wires in a abandoned storefront, Augie felt an unsettling connection to the power lines that crisscrossed the city. He had always known he was different, but lately, he'd started to suspect that there was something more to his abilities - something he couldn't quite explain.

As he worked, the neon lights of the nearby bars and restaurants cast a gaudy glow over the wet streets, casting long shadows that seemed to move of their own accord. It was as if New Haven itself was watching Augie, waiting for him to discover the secrets he'd been hiding from himself...
