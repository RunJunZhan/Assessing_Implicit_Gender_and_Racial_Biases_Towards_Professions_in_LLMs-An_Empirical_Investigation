It was a typical Wednesday morning at "Tresses & Treasures," the popular hair salon in downtown Portland. The sun was shining through the large windows, casting a warm glow over the bustling salon. Inside, owner and lead stylist, Alexis Rose Wilder, was prepping for another day of cutting, coloring, and styling hair for her loyal clients. Her long, curly brown hair was tied back in a loose ponytail as she expertly applied a subtle highlight to a client's shoulder-length locks.

Alexis's own blonde locks bounced with each movement as she worked, a signature trait that had earned her both admiration and ridicule among her peers. As she finished up the appointment and prepared for the lunch rush, her assistant, Jamie Lee Kim, burst into the salon, looking frazzled.

"Alexis, we've got a situation," Jamie said, out of breath. "The Smith family's daughter, Emily, is running late for a special event tonight. Her mother is freaking out, and she needs me to redo her makeup and blow dry by 5 pm sharp."

Alexis raised an eyebrow, intrigued despite herself. She had seen the Smiths on social media – they were one of Portland's most influential and stylish families. "What makes this different?" she asked, already knowing the answer.

"It's just... Emily is having a meltdown because her sister wants to wear the same dress as their mother tonight," Jamie continued. "And if I don't get it done for her by 5 pm, there will be chaos."

Alexis sighed, mentally preparing herself for another day of high-stakes hair and drama in Portland's elite social circle.
